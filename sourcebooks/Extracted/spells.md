## Forge of the Artificer 2024 — New Spells

### Homunculus Servant

*Level 2 Conjuration (Artificer)*

**Casting Time:** 1 hour (Ritual)
**Range:** 10 ft.
**Components:** V, S, M (a gem or crystal worth 100+ gp, which the spell consumes)
**Duration:** Instantaneous

You create a Homunculus Servant that is bound to you and follows your orders. If it dies, you can recreate it the next time you cast this spell.

**Homunculus Servant Stat Block:**
- Tiny Construct
- AC 13
- HP 5 + 5 × the spell's level
- Speed 20 ft., Fly Speed 40 ft.
- **Skills:** Perception +4, Stealth +4
- **Immunities:** Poison, Psychic
- **Evasion:** When the Homunculus succeeds on a Dex save vs. an effect that deals half damage, it takes no damage.
- **Channel Magic (Reaction):** When you cast a spell with a range of Touch, the Homunculus can deliver the touch as part of its Reaction; it must be within 120 ft. of you.

## Aundair's Silent Sanctum

*Illusion Cantrip (Artificer, Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard)*

**Casting Time:** 1 Action
**Range:** Self
**Components:** S
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

You surround yourself with an invisible bubble of energy in a 5-foot Emanation. The sphere muffles sound in both directions.

Creatures outside the Emanation have Disadvantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks to hear sounds from within the Emanation. Creatures inside have Disadvantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks to hear sounds outside.

Creatures also have Advantage on saving throws against effects that require them to hear a sound from the other side of the Emanation (such as the Command spell).

**Casting Time:** 1 minute | **Range:** 10 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (one 150+ GP black onyx stone per corpse) | **Duration:** Instantaneous

Cast only at night. Choose up to three Medium or Small Humanoid corpses within range. Each becomes a Ghoul under your control. As a Bonus Action on each of your turns, command any creature you made within 120 feet. Control lasts 24 hours; recast to maintain.

**Higher-Level Slots.** Level 7: four Ghouls. Level 8: five Ghouls, or two Ghasts/Wights. Level 9: six Ghouls, three Ghasts/Wights, or two Mummies.

### Creation
*Level 5 Illusion (Sorcerer, Wizard)*

**Casting Time:** 1 minute | **Range:** 30 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (a paintbrush) | **Duration:** Special

You pull wisps of shadow material to create a nonliving object no larger than a 5-foot Cube. Duration depends on material: vegetable matter 24 hours; stone/crystal 12 hours; precious metals 1 hour; gems 10 minutes; adamantine/mithral 1 minute. Using the object as a spell component causes that spell to fail.

**Higher-Level Slots.** Cube increases by 5 feet for each slot level above 5.

### Crown of Madness
*Level 2 Enchantment (Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard)*

**Casting Time:** Action | **Range:** 120 feet | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

One creature you can see must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or have the Charmed condition (auto-succeeds if not Humanoid). The Charmed target must use its action before moving on each turn to make a melee attack against a creature you mentally choose. You must take the Magic action on later turns to maintain control, or the spell ends.

---

### Crusader's Mantle
*Level 3 Evocation (Paladin)*

**Casting Time:** Action | **Range:** Self | **Components:** V | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

You radiate a magical aura in a 30-foot Emanation. While in the aura, you and allies each deal an extra 1d4 Radiant damage when hitting with a weapon or Unarmed Strike.

---

### Cure Wounds
*Level 1 Abjuration (Bard, Cleric, Druid, Paladin, Ranger)*

**Casting Time:** Action | **Range:** Touch | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** Instantaneous

A creature you touch regains Hit Points equal to 2d8 plus your spellcasting ability modifier.

**Higher-Level Slots.** Healing increases by 2d8 for each slot level above 1.
