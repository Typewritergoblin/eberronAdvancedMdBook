## PHB 2024 Species

### Aasimar
- **Creature Type:** Humanoid
- **Size:** Medium or Small
- **Speed:** 30 ft.
- **Source:** Player's Handbook 2024

**Traits:**
- **Celestial Resistance:** Resistance to Necrotic and Radiant damage.
- **Darkvision:** 60 ft.
- **Healing Hands:** Magic action; roll a number of d4s equal to your Proficiency Bonus and restore that many HP to one creature you touch. Once per Long Rest.
- **Light Bearer:** You know the *Light* cantrip. Charisma is the spellcasting ability.
- **Celestial Revelation (Level 3):** Bonus Action; transform for 1 minute. Choose one: Heavenly Wings (Fly Speed = Speed, spectral wings), Inner Radiance (Bright Light 10 ft/Dim 10 ft, end-of-turn Radiant damage to nearby creatures = PB), or Necrotic Shroud (Frighten nearby creatures, Necrotic damage = PB). Extra damage on the first attack each turn = Proficiency Bonus.

### Dragonborn
- **Creature Type:** Humanoid
- **Size:** Medium
- **Speed:** 30 ft.
- **Source:** Player's Handbook 2024

**Traits:**
- **Draconic Ancestry:** Choose a dragon type: Black (Acid), Blue (Lightning), Brass (Fire), Bronze (Lightning), Copper (Acid), Gold (Fire), Green (Poison), Red (Fire), Silver (Cold), White (Cold).
- **Breath Weapon:** As part of the Attack action, replace one attack with a breath weapon: 15-ft Cone or 30-ft Line 5-ft wide. Targets make Dex save (DC 8 + Con + PB) or take damage (type = ancestry). Damage: 1d10 at level 1, scaling to 4d10 at level 17. Uses = Proficiency Bonus (Short or Long Rest).
- **Damage Resistance:** Resistance to your ancestry's damage type.
- **Darkvision:** 60 ft.
- **Draconic Flight (Level 5):** Bonus Action; sprout spectral wings and gain Fly Speed = Speed for 10 minutes. Once per Long Rest.

### Shifter
- **Creature Type:** Humanoid
- **Size:** Medium or Small
- **Speed:** 30 ft.
- **Source:** Forge of the Artificer 2024

**Traits:**
- **Bestial Instincts:** Proficiency in two of the following: Acrobatics, Athletics, Intimidation, or Survival.

  - *Longtooth:* When you Shift, your teeth become natural weapons (Unarmed Strike; 1d6 + Str Piercing).

### Gargoyle

- **Creature Type:** Elemental
- **Size:** Medium (about 5–7 ft.)
- **Speed:** 30 ft.
- **Source:** Frontiers of Eberron 2024

*Carved from stone by Dhakaani goblins, brought to life by the daelkyr Orlassk. Oldest gargoyles have been in torpor for millennia. Common archetypes: Seekers, Guardians, Movers, Thinkers.*

**Traits:**
- **Darkvision:** 60 ft.
- **Elemental Nature:** Living stone provides:
  - Resistance to Poison damage; Advantage on saves vs. Poisoned.
  - No need to eat, drink, or breathe. Immune to Dehydration/Malnutrition/Suffocation Exhaustion.
  - Immune to disease.
  - No need to sleep; immune to magical sleep.
  - Cannot be subjected to the Petrified condition against your will.
- **False Appearance:** While motionless, indistinguishable from an inanimate statue.
- **Sentry's Rest:** Long Rest in 6 hours if spent inactive and motionless (while retaining consciousness).
- **Stoneskin:** Natural armor. While not wearing armor, base AC = **13 + Dexterity modifier**. Can use a Shield.

### Worg

- **Creature Type:** Monstrosity
- **Size:** Medium (about 4–5 ft.)
- **Speed:** 40 ft.
- **Source:** Frontiers of Eberron 2024

*Canid predators with sharp intelligence and speech. Worgs of the Watching Wood are devoted to the Devourer and the Fury. More compact than other strains; can carry a Medium rider.*

**Traits:**
- **Darkvision:** 60 ft.
- **Keen Senses:** Proficiency in Perception; Advantage on Perception checks using hearing or smell.
- **Bite:** Natural weapon (Unarmed Strike): **1d8 + Strength modifier Piercing** damage.
- **For the Pack:** You can use the **Help action as a Bonus Action**.
- **Natural Armor:** While not wearing armor, base AC = **12 + Dexterity modifier**.
- **Quadruped:** Your quadrupedal form has these traits:
  - Cannot hold items in paws or wield weapons/Shields (exception: Steel Jaws — a special Martial Melee weapon, 2d6 Finesse, Topple mastery, 2 lb., 10 GP; wielded by clenching over teeth).
  - Can perform Somatic spell components with front paws.
  - Disadvantage on ability checks requiring fine manipulation.
  - Can carry a rider of Medium size or smaller; count as one size larger for carrying capacity.
  - Must wear barding (4× humanoid armor cost, 2× weight) instead of armor.
  - Climbing requiring hands costs 4 extra feet per foot of movement.

## Frontiers of Eberron — New Cantrips (Species-Related)

### Gray Gaze

*Transmutation Cantrip (Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock; also granted to Medusa species)*

**Casting Time:** Bonus Action | **Range:** 20 ft. | **Components:** V, S, M (a pebble with a hole in it) | **Duration:** Instantaneous

Target one creature within range. If the target can see your eyes, it makes a **Constitution saving throw** or takes Necrotic damage equal to your **Proficiency Bonus** and has **Disadvantage on the next Dexterity saving throw** it makes before the end of its next turn.

### Orien Step

*Transmutation Cantrip (Mark of Passage only)*

**Casting Time:** Bonus Action | **Range:** Self | **Components:** S | **Duration:** Instantaneous

Teleport up to **5 feet** to an unoccupied space you can see. Cannot teleport through solid objects or out of manacles/similar restraints.

## Aasimar Variants

Standard Aasimar traits apply, with the following replacements depending on origin.

### Fernian Aasimar

Infused with otherworldly fire from the plane of Fernia. When you use Celestial Revelation, your Heavenly Wings are formed from flame; your Necrotic Shroud is a terrifying aura of ghostly fire; your Inner Radiance deals Fire damage instead of Radiant.

Replace Celestial Resistance and Light Bearer with:

**Fernian Resistance.** You have Resistance to Fire damage and Necrotic damage.

**Flame Bearer.** You know the Produce Flame cantrip. Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma is your spellcasting ability (choose when you select this species).

### Mabaran Aasimar

Drawn from shadows and despair, tied to the plane of Mabar. Replace the Light Bearer trait with Herald of Death, and replace the Inner Radiance option of Celestial Revelation with Inner Darkness.

**Herald of Death.** You know the Toll the Dead cantrip. Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma is your spellcasting ability (choose when you select this species).

**Inner Darkness.** You consume light and life, creating a shroud of shadows. For the duration, you reduce Bright Light in a 20-foot radius to Dim Light, and at the end of each of your turns, each creature within 10 feet of you takes Necrotic damage equal to your Proficiency Bonus.

## Dhakaani Ghaal'dar (Hobgoblin)

The ghaal'dar ("mighty folk") are soldiers, scholars, and leaders of the Dhakaani Empire. They are valued for their versatility and vision.

## Ruinbound

Mutants born to any humanoid species with contact with the Daelkyr or their minions. A Ruinbound is born with a personal symbiont permanently fused to their body. Many have additional disturbing mutations (cosmetic only, no mechanical effect).

**Creature Type:** Humanoid
**Size:** Medium or Small (choose when you select this species)
**Speed:** 30 feet

**Darkvision.** Darkvision with range of 120 feet.

**Personal Symbiont.** You are bound to an alien entity that is part of your body and cannot be removed. Choose a cantrip: Acid Splash, Guidance (self only), Light, Mage Hand, Mind Sliver, Poison Spray, Resistance (self only), Shocking Grasp, Thorn Whip. Your symbiont can use this cantrip on your behalf. You can switch to a different cantrip from this list when you finish a Long Rest. Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma is your spellcasting ability (choose when you select this species).

**Symbiont Mastery.** You can attune to one magic item with the Symbiotic Nature property without it counting toward your attunement maximum. Whenever you finish a Long Rest, you can end your Attunement to a Symbiotic Nature item, ignoring any restrictions requiring Remove Curse or similar magic.

**Unnatural Resilience.** Resistance to Poison damage. Advantage on saving throws to avoid or end the Poisoned condition.

## Sahuagin

Ancient species spawned in the ocean depths, kin to the shark, often devotees of the Devourer. Natural predators with aggressive instincts, but not innately evil — in the Thunder Sea, sahuagin champions battle fiends and aberrations.

Sahuagin have armored skin, sharp teeth, webbed hands and feet, and finlike crests on their heads that extend and retract with mood. Scales range from gray-green to dramatic colorful patterns.

**Creature Type:** Humanoid
**Size:** Medium (about 5–7 feet tall)
**Speed:** 30 feet

**Darkvision.** Darkvision with range of 120 feet.

**Swift Swimmer.** You have a Swim Speed equal to your Speed.

**Blood Frenzy.** As a Bonus Action, you can enter a blood frenzy for 1 minute. During this time, you have Advantage on attack rolls against any creature that doesn't have all its Hit Points. Uses equal to your Proficiency Bonus; all recharge on Long Rest.

**Natural Armor.** When not wearing armor, your base AC equals 12 + Dexterity modifier. You can use a Shield and still gain this benefit.

**Limited Amphibiousness.** You can breathe air and water, but you need to be submerged at least once every 4 hours to avoid suffocating.
