## Frontiers of Eberron 2024 — Magic Items

### Beggar's Wisdom

*Wondrous Item, Common*

A small leatherbound book of the wit and wisdom of Beggar Dane, enchanted. As a Magic action while holding the book, declare your desire for assistance with a particular task; the book opens to a relevant quip. You add **1d4** to the next ability check you make within the next minute. Once used, no copy of *Beggar's Wisdom* can benefit you again until next dawn.

### Daff's Wonderful Draught

*Potion, Uncommon*

As a Bonus Action, drink it or administer to a creature within 5 feet. The creature rolls d6 to determine the effect:

| d6 | Effect |
|---|---|
| 1–3 | Regain **4d4 + 4** Hit Points. |
| 4 | Gain effects of *Detect Thoughts* for 1 minute (Concentration; save DC 13). |
| 5 | Gain effects of *Haste* for 1 minute (Concentration). |
| 6 | DC 13 Wisdom save or suffer *Tasha's Hideous Laughter* for 1 minute. |

### Dry Spell

*Scroll, Rare*

A desiccated Spell Scroll with ineffable cipher. As a Magic action, cast *Control Water* without Material components (save DC 15). Additionally, any water manipulated by the spell is **destroyed** when the spell ends. Single use — scroll crumbles after casting. If casting is interrupted, scroll is not lost.

### Duelist's Scorecard

*Wondrous Item, Common*

A scrap of paper with space for five names. Write a creature's name in a blank space; the paper shuffles text to show that creature's dueling ranking (relative to others listed), based on duels won in the past year (sourced from the Infinite Archive of Daanvi). When all five spaces are filled, no new rankings can be revealed for **1 week**, after which all names are magically erased.

### Flashdraw Wand

*Wand, Uncommon (Requires Attunement)*

While attuned to this Wand:
- You **cannot be Surprised**.
- Whenever you roll Initiative, you can immediately draw this wand (free). If you do, roll **1d4** and add it to your Initiative score; all creatures within 5 feet of you take **1 Lightning** damage.

### Jury's Squeaky Wheel

A magic Shield shaped like a wooden wagon wheel, crafted by Jury d'Cannith of Quickstone.

As a **Bonus Action** while holding this Shield, grant **Temporary Hit Points equal to your level** to the humanoid creature you can see within 60 feet with the fewest current HP (your choice if tied).

### Lightning Runner

*Wondrous Item, Uncommon*

A 10-ft × 10-ft × 6-in wooden and steel platform that levitates 5 ft off the ground near lightning rail conductor stones (within 20 ft of an operational track). Carries up to **2,000 pounds** (drops to ground if overloaded).

**Powered Movement:** As a Magic action, a creature touching the pump can expend a 1st-level or higher spell slot to move the platform 30 ft/turn forward or backward along the track for 1 hour, or until stopped with another Magic action. A creature with the Mark of Passage does not need to expend a spell slot.

### Quickstone Boomstick

*Rod, Uncommon (Requires Attunement)*

When you cast a spell of 1st level or higher using this Rod as a Spellcasting Focus, you can choose to release a thunderous burst. All creatures within 5 feet of you (including you) must make a **Con save** vs. your spell save DC. On a failed save:
- Take **1d4 Thunder damage per level of the spell cast**.
- **Deafened** until end of their next turn.

### Sandstorm Staff

*Staff, Uncommon (Requires Attunement)*

Magic Quarterstaff. **10 charges**, regains 1d6 + 4 expended charges daily at dawn.

When you cast a spell using this Staff as Spellcasting Focus, expend 1 charge to gain a **+1 bonus to all spell attack rolls** for that spell. The Staff also conjures a **5-ft diameter dust vortex** in your space.

**Dust vortex behavior** (at start of each of your turns, roll d20 per vortex):
- Roll 11+: vortex moves 5 ft in a random direction.
- Roll 1–10: vortex disappears.

Any creature that starts its turn within 5 ft of a vortex makes a **Con save** vs. your spell save DC. On fail: **1d4 Bludgeoning + Blinded** until end of its turn.

### Shock and Ore

*Weapon (pair of War Picks), Rare (Requires Attunement), Unique*

Two War Picks treated as a single magic item. Benefits while wielding both:

- **+1** to attack and damage rolls.

### Stonebane Weapon

*Weapon (Any Melee), Uncommon*

Forged from **byeshk** by Dhakaani artificers to combat minions of the daelkyr Orlassk. Does not corrode.

- **Stone Penetration:** Ignores Resistance to nonmagical BPS damage when used against stone creatures, creatures with stone-like natural armor (e.g., Gargoyles), and Petrified creatures.
- **Byeshk Effect:** On hit vs. an Aberration: **+1d6 damage** (same type as weapon) + target **can't regain HP** until start of your next turn.

> **Byeshk Weapons (general rule):** Melee weapons of byeshk ignore normal resistances vs. Aberrations. Cost: **+400 GP** over normal version. These weapons are **not inherently magical** but have the Aberration-targeting property.

### Three-Faced Coin

*Wondrous Item, Common*

A gold piece with a crown of Galifar on one face and the Octogram on the other — plus a hidden third face. When flipped, it can land on three faces corresponding to patrons of the Three Faces of Coin: **Kol Korran, Onatar**, or the **Keeper**.

When flipped, you can consciously **will the coin to land on a face of your choice**. Once used, can't do so again until next dawn. After being flipped, the coin returns to two faces when next touched.

## Fiendish Treasures

*Items crafted from demonglass — solidified fiendish essence crystallized by the Silver Flame during the binding of the overlords. Raw demonglass is pure solidified evil (indestructible, radiates malevolence). Bound demonglass contains the essence of a specific native fiend.*

*All demonglass items register as Fiends when detected with Detect Evil and Good or Divine Sense.*

### Brand of Tyranny

*Rod, Very Rare (Requires Attunement)*

A heavy, black iron Rod that glows with sickly purple light.

While holding: **+1 bonus** to spell attack rolls and spell save DCs.

**Touch a creature:** That creature makes a **DC 15 Charisma saving throw** or be **branded with the mark of Tol Kharash** (the Horned King). While branded, whenever the creature makes an attack roll or a save against being Frightened, it rolls **1d4 and subtracts** from the roll. Brand lasts until next dawn, *Remove Curse*, or *Greater Restoration*.

A demonglass shard is inlaid into this weapon.

- **+1** to attack and damage rolls.

### Demonglass Orb

*Orb, Rare (Requires Attunement)*

Red and black flames flicker within a rough-cut demonglass shard.

When casting a **Divination spell** using this Orb as Spellcasting Focus, you can sacrifice life force to weaken a target's save: roll **1–4d6** (decide before DM announces success/fail). You take that much **Necrotic damage** (unreducible). The target's saving throw is **reduced by 1 per d6 rolled**.

### Kharash's Promise

*Wondrous Item, Instrument, Artifact (Requires Attunement)*

A 3-foot warhorn forged of demonglass and black iron; a trickle of boiling blood constantly drips from its wide end. Created by Tol Kharash (the Horned King, overlord of tyranny) to announce his coming across the Barrens.

- **+3** to spell attack rolls and Bard spell save DCs.
- **2 minor beneficial + 1 major beneficial + 2 minor detrimental** random Artifact properties.
- **Surety of Dominance:** You cannot be Charmed or Frightened. If a creature targets you with a Charm/Fright magical effect, that creature takes **4d10 Psychic** damage.
- **Thaumaturgic Clarion:** The horn can be heard up to **1 mile** away when blown.
- **Promised End (Magic action, 13-day recharge):** Blow the horn and target one creature that can see and hear it. **DC 20 Charisma save.** On fail: target's **HP maximum is reduced by 1 at start of each of its turns** until it dies, you die, you're on different planes, or the effect is ended by *Wish*. The target can use a Bonus Action while affected to teleport to within 5 feet of you.

## Symbionts

*Living tools and weapons created by the daelkyr, formed from chitin, bone, and muscle. Treated as magic items mechanically. Many have Symbiotic Nature: can't be removed while attuned; can't voluntarily end Attunement; can only be ended by Remove Curse or equivalent.*

---

### Ink's Aide

*Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement), Unique*

A colorful, jellyfish-like symbiont cultivated by Quickstone schoolmaster "Ink" Narathun. Attune by holding it to one ear for the full Attunement period (tentacles enter ear canal; does not impair hearing).

- **4 charges** (regains 1d4 at dawn).
- **Comprehend Languages** (1 charge; save DC 15) — Magic action.
- **Enhance Ability** (2 charges; save DC 15) — Magic action.
- **Symbiotic Nature:** Can't be removed while attuned; can't voluntarily end Attunement. *Remove Curse* ends the Attunement.

---

### Living Stoneskin

*Heavy Armor (Plate Armor), Very Rare (Requires Attunement)*

Plate Armor of stone slabs carved with alien symbols. Attune by wearing it for the full Attunement period (stone plates fuse with skin).

- **+1 bonus** to Armor Class.
- **Resistance** to nonmagical Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing damage.
- **Hardening:** If you take 20+ nonmagical BPS damage from one roll (after Resistance), you are **Petrified until end of your next turn**.
- **Symbiotic Nature:** Can't be removed while attuned; can't voluntarily end Attunement. *Remove Curse* ends it.
- **Feeding the Armor:** After each Long Rest, feed **half your remaining Hit Dice** (round up) to the armor, or take **1 level of Exhaustion**.

---

### Orlassk's Reach

*Wondrous Item, Artifact (Requires Attunement)*

The lost arm of the daelkyr Orlassk. Attune by holding it to one arm for the full Attunement period (stone skin softens and envelops your limb).

- **2 minor beneficial + 2 minor detrimental + 1 major beneficial + 1 major detrimental** random Artifact properties.
- **Voice of the Daelkyr:** Speak with any Aberration as if sharing a language.
- **Command Aberration (Magic action, once per dawn):** Cast *Dominate Monster* on an Aberration (save DC 18).
- **Petrifying Touch (passive):** Any creature you touch with your stone fingertips is affected by *Flesh to Stone* (save DC 18). You cannot concentrate on or suppress this effect.
- **Stone Speaking (Magic action):** Imbue stones in a 30-ft Emanation with limited sentience; question them about events in the past day. Can communicate with earth elementals and Petrified creatures in the area (no magical influence).
- **Greater Symbiotic Nature:** Can only be ended by a *Wish* spell.

---

### Quickstone Whisperer

*Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement)*

An ancient, heavy marble torc that coils around your throat during Attunement.

- **Magic action:** Transform a **5-ft cube of quickstone** between its soft/wet form, or irreversibly change it into its fixed form.
- **Mold Earth (at will):** As an Action, cast *Mold Earth* (XGE).
- **Meld into Stone (once per dusk):** As an Action, cast *Meld into Stone*.
- **Symbiotic Nature:** Can't be removed while attuned; can't voluntarily end Attunement. *Remove Curse* ends it.

*Wondrous Item, Very Rare*

## Common Magic Items (Everyday Eberron)

### Battle Rod
*Rod, Common*
Held in two hands: cast cantrips at up to twice standard range. When cast beyond standard range, attack rolls have Disadvantage and targets have Advantage on saving throws against the cantrip.

### Charlatan's Gloves
*Wondrous Item, Common*
Magic action: produce an illusory image fitting in the palm of your hand (lasts 1 minute or until Concentration ends). Can't create sound, light, smell, or other sensory effects. Physical interaction reveals the illusion. Creatures can make an Intelligence (Investigation) check contested by your Charisma (Deception) check to discern it.

### Drybrooch
*Wondrous Item, Common*
Magic action: summon or dismiss a dome of energy above your head that repels moderate quantities of frozen and liquid water (rain, snow, droppings). Cannot protect against harsh environmental hazards or attacks.

### Duster
*Wand, Common*
Magic action: instantly clean an area within 10 feet no larger than 1 cubic foot. Variants include the Staff of Cleansing (five-foot cube) and the Bright Basin (for cloth objects).

### Integrated Tool
*Wondrous Item, Common (Requires Use by a Warforged)*
Attaches to a warforged's body, functioning as any tool weighing 10 lbs or less (Artisan's Tool, Forgery Kit, Herbalism Kit, Poisoner's Kit, Thieves' Tools, or Musical Instrument). Takes 1 hour to attach or remove. Cannot be removed against your will while alive. Only one Integrated Tool at a time.

### Lamplighter
*Wand, Common*
Magic action: instantaneously light or snuff out a candle, torch, small campfire, or similar flame source within 10 feet.

### Spark
*Wondrous Item, Common*
Small oval stone engraved with Fernian glyphs. Bonus Action: touch a candle, torch, or similar object to instantly ignite it.

### Talking Wand
*Wand, Common*
While holding, your voice and sounds you produce are three times as loud as normal. House Phiarlan variant: Thunderous Amulet (hands free).

### Thurimbar Rod
*Musical Instrument, Common*
Magic action: produce auditory illusions that replicate the sound of any instrument you are proficient with. Sound emanates from the rod. Proficient users can produce unearthly music unlike any natural instrument.

### War Staff
*Staff, Common (Requires Attunement by a Spellcaster)*
While holding in both hands (used as Arcane Focus): cantrips can be cast at up to four times standard range. When cast beyond twice standard range, attack rolls have Disadvantage and targets have Advantage on saving throws.

## Dhakaani Magic Items

### Breastplate of Kamvuul Norek
*Armor (Breastplate), Legendary (Requires Attunement)*
Adamantine breastplate of the ancient Dhakaani. +2 bonus to AC. Any Critical Hit against you becomes a normal hit. Advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects. If you succeed on a save against a Mind Flayer or other Aberration's Mind Blast, you are unaffected and the effect is reflected back toward the Aberration (as if the cone originated from you).

### Ghaal'duur, the Mighty Dirge
*Wondrous Item, Artifact (Requires Attunement by a Bard)*
Three-foot warhorn carved from a red dragon's horn tip, banded with adamantine, covered with runes of power. Crafted by Jhazaal Dhakaan, a legendary dirge singer who united the ancient Dhakaani clans.

**Random Properties:** 2 minor beneficial properties; 1 minor detrimental property (goblinoids attuned to the warhorn are immune to the detrimental effects).

**Jhazaal's Presence.** Your Charisma increases by 4 (max 24).

**The Dragon's Song.** Used as a Spellcasting Focus for Bard spells:
- Duration of 1 min+ is doubled (max 24 hours)
- Range of 5 ft+ is doubled
- Spells of 1st level or higher are cast as if from a slot 1 level higher

**The Sound of Victory.** Magic action: blow the horn to empower allies and intimidate foes. For 10 minutes: all allies within 600 feet who can hear you add 1d4 (or 2d4 for goblinoids) to attack rolls and saving throws. All enemies within 600 feet who can hear must make a Charisma saving throw (your Bard spell save DC); those who fail subtract 1d4 (or 2d4 for goblinoids) from attack rolls and saving throws for 10 minutes. Once per Short or Long Rest.

**Destroying the Horn.** Only the fire of Jharaashta, the dragon who sacrificed his horn, can destroy it.

### Grieving Tree
*Eldritch Machine (Requires Attunement Using Lost Techniques)*
Giant skeletal tree of white marble. Part execution device, part torture device. Creation secrets were lost with the daashor Taruuzh.

**Lingering Life.** Pool of Hit Points (starts at 20, max 200). While the pool has HP, imprisoned creatures at 0 HP are healed 1d4+1 HP (same amount deducted from pool).

**Prisoners.** Holds up to 4 creatures. Freeing a prisoner: DC 20 Strength (Athletics) check, both take 3d6 Piercing on success; both take damage on failure as well.

**Tree Control (while attuned, within 120 ft):**
- **Grab (Magic action):** DC 24 Dex save; failed: captured, 6d6 Piercing, Stunned until released.
- **Eviscerate (Magic action):** All Stunned prisoners take 8d6 Piercing; half total damage added to Lingering Life pool.
- **Answer for Their Crimes (Magic action):** Cast Speak with Dead on an imprisoned corpse without components; corpse is compelled to speak truthfully.
- **Release a Prisoner (Bonus Action):** Release one prisoner.

### Keeper's Fang
*Weapon (Dagger), Rare*
+1 bonus to attack and damage rolls. Whenever this dagger reduces a creature to 0 Hit Points, the creature is slain and its soul removed. A creature whose soul is stolen can only be restored to life by a Wish spell.

### Shaarat'doovol, the Blade of Truth
*Weapon (Longsword), Legendary (Requires Attunement)*
Forged in khaar'draguus (lava pits below Sharn) by an ancient Dhakaani hero who fell against the daelkyr Belashyrra. A sentience resides in the blade; it communicates through emotions.

+3 bonus to attack and damage rolls. When you hit an Aberration with this weapon, it takes an extra 2d10 Radiant damage.

**Unyielding Duty.** If you have the favor of the sword, while wielding it:
- **Truesight.** Truesight with range of 90 feet.
- **Fortified Mind.** Immunity to Charmed and Frightened conditions; Resistance to Psychic damage.

### Uul'kur (Dream Key)
*Wondrous Item, Common*
Iron token inlaid with a Siberys dragonshard sliver. Usually worn as a brooch, coin, or key. Known only to the chot'uul monks of the Heirs of Dhakaan.

While an Uul'kur is on your person when you sleep, you remain fully lucid in dreams and remember everything upon waking. If you understand the workings of the Uul'kur, you can take a Magic action to bind it to your dreams until the next dawn; while bound, any creature who sleeps with this token on their person shares your dream instead of creating their own.

### Vola'khesh
*Wondrous Item, Common*
Small carved stone worn on a leather strap. Created in linked sets (up to 10 stones per set); once linked, cannot be changed. Currently only used by the Kech Dhakaan.

Bonus Action: whisper a message heard by all creatures within 120 feet wearing a linked Vola'khesh. Blocked by magical silence, 1 foot of stone, 1 inch of common metal, or 3 feet of wood. Can travel around corners and through openings.

## General Byeshk Weapons
*Nonmagical Weapon Enhancement*
Melee weapons made of or coated with byeshk (a rare purple metal from the Byeshk and Graywall Mountains) deal an extra 1d6 damage of the weapon's type whenever they hit an Aberration, and that Aberration can't regain Hit Points until the start of your next turn. Cost: 400 GP more than the normal version.

## Symbionts (Daelkyr Creations)

Symbionts are living tools and weapons formed from chitin, bone, and muscle. They are mechanically considered magic items, not creatures.

### Crawling Gauntlet
*Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement)*
Surrounds your hand and forearm when attuned. Unarmed Strikes can slash with talons: 1d6 + Strength modifier Slashing damage. Magic action: cast Mage Hand using an ectoplasmic duplicate of the gauntlet.

**Symbiotic Nature.** Cannot be removed unless targeted by Remove Curse or similar magic.

### Coat of Many Eyes
*Armor (Studded Leather), Uncommon/Rare/Very Rare (evolves with service to Belashyrra; Requires Attunement)*
Created by the daelkyr Belashyrra, covered with different types of eyes. All rarities grant:
- **Darkvision** with range of 120 feet
- **Eyes Everywhere:** Advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight

**Rare:** Also grants +1 bonus to AC and the ability to see Invisible creatures and objects within 120 feet.

**Very Rare:** Also grants +1 bonus to AC, see Invisible within 120 feet, and Magic action: cast Arcane Eye (once per Long Rest).

**Symbiotic Nature.** Cannot be removed unless targeted by Remove Curse or similar magic.

### Hungry Weapon
*Weapon (Any Melee), Uncommon (Requires Attunement)*
Daelkyr weapon of muscle and bone, moans/growls/wails; twists in grip if it doesn't like you. Non-attuned wielders have Disadvantage on attacks with it. When another creature tries to wield it while you're attuned, extruding thorns deal 1d4 Piercing damage each turn.

Bonus Action: bond to the weapon (tendrils burrow into your flesh). Magic action to end. While bonded:
- **Secure Grip.** Weapon bound to hand; cannot be removed voluntarily or involuntarily.
- **Drain Life.** When you hit, you can expend 1 Hit Die: roll it + Con modifier. The attack deals extra Necrotic damage equal to result, and you regain that many Hit Points.

### Shadow Sibling
*Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement)*
Black jewel-like object that fuses to center of forehead when attuned. Bonus Action: exude or retract an ectoplasmic shadow around your body. While active: Disadvantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks to see you; Advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks to Hide.

Reaction to an attack: expend 1 Hit Die, roll it, add result to AC (including against the triggering attack). The bonus lasts until end of your next turn.

**Symbiotic Nature.** Cannot be removed unless targeted by Remove Curse or similar magic.

### Spellburrow
*Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement)*
Scarab-like symbiont bred by Valaara. Burrows through skull while attuning, establishing a psychic connection. Possesses alien sentience.

Knows one cantrip (DM's choice, Sorcerer or Warlock spell list); you can cast it as if you know it. Also knows one level 1 spell and one level 2 spell from the same list. If you have Spellcasting or Pact Magic, these count as prepared (don't count against your prepared spells limit). Spellcasting ability: Intelligence modifier, or any spellcasting ability modifier you have from another source.

**Symbiotic Nature.** Cannot be removed unless targeted by Remove Curse or similar magic.

### Throwing Scarab
*Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement)*
Bred by Valaara; fuses with one of your hands when attuned. For every attack as part of the Attack action on your turn, you can extrude a shard of razor-sharp chitin — a magical Simple Melee weapon (Finesse, Light, Thrown; 20 ft/60 ft range). On a hit: 1d4 Piercing + 1d4 Acid damage. The shard dissolves immediately after hitting or missing.

**Symbiotic Nature.** Cannot be removed unless targeted by Remove Curse or similar magic.

### Tongueworm
*Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement)*
Attaches to your throat and nestles under your tongue. Bonus Action: Unarmed Strike using Strength or Dexterity, dealing 1d4 + Str/Dex modifier Piercing damage. Target makes DC 13 Constitution saving throw or is Poisoned until the start of your next turn.

**Symbiotic Nature.** Cannot be removed unless targeted by Remove Curse or similar magic.

### Wandering Eye
*Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement)*
A leathery tentacle with a bulbous eye at one end; burrows into one of your shoulders. While the eye is uncovered:

**Always Vigilant.** The eye remains watchful during your Long Rest; use your Passive Perception to notice threats. The eye sends a nightmare alarm if it spots danger, waking you immediately.

**Periscopic Vision.** The eye can stretch up to 18 inches to peer around corners or over cover (AC 20, 6 HP while extended; if reduced to 0 HP, benefits unavailable until Short or Long Rest).

**Third Eye.** When making a Wisdom (Perception) or Intelligence (Investigation) check involving sight, you can roll 1d4 and add it to the check.

**Symbiotic Nature.** Cannot be removed unless targeted by Remove Curse or similar magic.

## Siberys Dragonmarks (Supernatural Gifts)

Siberys Dragonmarks are massive dragonmarks that appear unexpectedly and grant exceptional power. They are Supernatural Gifts granted by the DM. A character cannot possess more than one, and cannot have a Siberys Dragonmark while also having a Dragonmark feat associated with a different dragonmark.

All Siberys Marks share the **Perfect Intuition** benefit (Advantage on ability checks with a specific skill, as noted per mark) and a powerful spell.

### Mark of Detection
**Perfect Intuition:** Advantage on Intelligence (Investigation) checks.
**Truest Sight:** Always have True Seeing prepared. Cast once without slot (recharge Long Rest). Spellcasting ability: Int, Wis, or Cha (choose once).

### Mark of Finding
**Perfect Intuition:** Advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks.
**Finder's Focus:** Always have Commune with Nature and Find the Path prepared. Cast each once without slot (recharge Short Rest). Spellcasting ability: Int, Wis, or Cha (choose once).

### Mark of Handling

**Powerful Influence:** Always have Dominate Beast prepared. Cast once without slot (recharge Long Rest). When cast this way: Advantage on Concentration checks, can concentrate up to 8 hours (as if L7 slot cast), and can maintain while sleeping (Disadvantage on Concentration check if you take damage while asleep). Spellcasting ability: Int, Wis, or Cha (choose once).

### Mark of Healing
**Perfect Intuition:** Advantage on Wisdom (Medicine) checks.
**Miracle Worker:** Always have Heal prepared. Cast once without slot (recharge Long Rest). Spellcasting ability: Int, Wis, or Cha (choose once).

### Mark of Making

**Master Crafter:** Always have Fabricate prepared. Cast once without slot (recharge Short Rest).
