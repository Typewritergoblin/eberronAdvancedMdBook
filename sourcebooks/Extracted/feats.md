## Forge of the Artificer 2024 — Dragonmark Feats

All Dragonmark feats share the prerequisite: **Eberron Campaign Setting**. You can't have more than one Dragonmark feat unless specified (Aberrant Dragonmark has separate rules from the House marks).

---

### Aberrant Dragonmark

*Origin Feat (Eberron Campaign)*

**Aberrant Fortitude.** When you fail a Constitution saving throw, you can take a Reaction to roll 1d4 and add it to the result. If this turns the failure into a success, that's the new result. Once per Long Rest.

**Aberrant Magic.** Learn one Sorcerer cantrip and one level-1 Sorcerer spell of your choice. The spell can be cast once per Short or Long Rest without a spell slot. Constitution is your spellcasting ability.

**Aberrant Surge.** When you cast the level-1 spell from Aberrant Magic, you can expend one Hit Die as part of casting: roll the die; on an even result, gain Temporary HP equal to the roll; on an odd result, deal Force damage equal to the roll to one creature within 30 ft.

## Forge of the Artificer 2024 — Greater Dragonmark Feats

*General Feats (Prerequisite: Level 4+, corresponding Dragonmark feat)*

Each Greater Mark feat grants:
- **+1 to one ability score** (ability tied to the mark's core skill)
- **Improved Intuition:** The mark's intuition bonus increases to **1d6**.
- **Unique enhanced ability** (listed below per mark).

| Feat | Unique Benefit |
|---|---|
| Greater Aberrant Mark | Aberrant Surge damage/healing increases to 2d4; Aberrant Magic spell can now also be cast with a spell slot. |
| Greater Mark of Detection | Shared Detection: Share results of *Detect Magic* or *Detect Thoughts* with allies within 60 ft. |
| Greater Mark of Finding | Finder's Pursuit: Cast *Locate Creature* once per Long Rest without a slot. |
| Greater Mark of Handling | Beast Master's Call: Extra use of Primal Connection (total 2/Long Rest). |
| Greater Mark of Healing | Healer's Gift: When casting a healing spell, target also ends one condition (Blinded, Deafened, or Poisoned). |
| Greater Mark of Hospitality | Innkeeper's Welcome: Cast *Heroes' Feast* once per Long Rest. |
| Greater Mark of Making | Maker's Gift: Crafting time for non-magical items is halved. |
| Greater Mark of Passage | Swift Passage: When you cast *Misty Step*, you can bring one willing creature of your size or smaller with you. |
| Greater Mark of Scribing | Master Scribe: Cast *Sending* once per Long Rest without a slot. |
| Greater Mark of Sentinel | Guardian's Call: Can use Vigilant Guardian when the ally is within 10 ft. (up from 5 ft.). |
| Greater Mark of Shadow | Shadow Dance: While Invisible (from Shape Shadows), can attack without ending Invisibility once per turn. |
| Greater Mark of Storm | Tempest's Might: Once per Short or Long Rest, cast *Lightning Bolt* without a slot. |
| Greater Mark of Warding | Lockmaster: *Knock* is now free (no slot, unlimited uses). |

## Forgotten Realms Heroes of Faerûn 2024 — Origin Feats

---

### Cult of the Dragon Initiate

*Origin Feat*

**Dragon's Tongue.** Learn Draconic (or another language if you already know it).

**Dragon's Terror.** Magic action; instill terror in one creature within 30 ft. (Wis save DC 8 + Wis + PB). On fail: Frightened until end of your next turn. If they succeed or the effect ends, they're immune for 24 hours.

**Inspired by Fear.** When you cause a creature to have the Frightened condition, gain Heroic Inspiration if you lack it. Once per Short or Long Rest.

---

### Emerald Enclave Fledgling

*Origin Feat*

**Speak with Animals.** Always prepared; cast with spell slots. When cast as a Ritual, duration is 8 hours. Choose Int, Wis, or Cha as spellcasting ability.

**Tag Team.** When you take the Help action, you can swap places with a willing ally within 5 ft. (no Opportunity Attacks; ally can't be Incapacitated).

---

### Harper Agent

*Origin Feat*

**Thieves' Cant.** You know Thieves' Cant.

**Instrument Training.** Proficiency with one Musical Instrument of your choice.

**Distracting Melody.** When you take the Help action to assist an ally's attack roll, the enemy you're distracting can be within 30 ft. of you (not just within 5 ft.), provided it can see or hear you.

---

### Lords' Alliance Agent

*Origin Feat*

**Inspiring Strike.** Once per turn when you score a Critical Hit, choose an ally within 30 ft. without Heroic Inspiration — that ally gains Heroic Inspiration.

**Reassert Honor.** When an enemy deals damage to your ally within 5 ft. of you, gain Advantage on your next attack roll against that enemy before the end of your next turn.

---

### Purple Dragon Rook

*Origin Feat*

**Entreat.** Proficiency in one of: Insight, Performance, or Persuasion.

**Rallying Cry.** When you roll Initiative and aren't Incapacitated, choose up to PB creatures within 30 ft. that you can see — they gain Heroic Inspiration. Once per Long Rest.

---

### Spellfire Spark

*Origin Feat*

**Magic Absorption.** Once per turn, when you take damage from a spell or magical effect, reduce the total by 1d4 (can't use while Incapacitated).

**Spellfire Flame.** Learn the *Sacred Flame* cantrip (Int, Wis, or Cha). Cast as a Bonus Action PB times per day (Long Rest to recharge).

---

### Tyro of the Gauntlet

*Origin Feat*

**Stand as One.** When an ally within 5 ft. would be pushed or pulled, take a Reaction to prevent it (ally can't be Incapacitated).

**Vigilant.** When you take the Ready action, the next attack roll against you has Disadvantage before the start of your next turn.

---

### Zhentarim Ruffian

*Origin Feat*

**Exploit Opening.** When you roll damage for an Opportunity Attack, roll the damage dice twice and use either roll.

**Family First.** If you have Heroic Inspiration when you roll Initiative, expend it to give all allies Advantage on that Initiative roll.

### Cold Caster

*General Feat (Level 4+)*

**+1 Int, Wis, or Cha (max 20).**

**Cantrip.** Learn *Ray of Frost* (or another Wizard cantrip if you already know it). Spellcasting ability = the ability increased.

**Frostbite.** Once per turn when you hit a creature and deal Cold damage, the creature subtracts 1d4 from the next saving throw it makes before the end of your next turn.

---

### Dragonscarred

*General Feat (Level 4+, requires Cult of the Dragon Initiate)*

**+1 Constitution or Charisma (max 20).**

**Damage Resistance.** Choose one: Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Poison. Gain Resistance to it.

**Fearsome Power.** When you deal damage as part of the Attack or Magic action, you can use Dragon's Terror (from Cult of Dragon Initiate) as a Bonus Action that turn.

---

### Enclave Magic

*General Feat (Level 4+, requires Emerald Enclave Fledgling)*

**+1 Int, Wis, or Cha (max 20).**

**Friend to Animals.** Advantage on ability checks when taking the Influence action with Beasts.

**Two Hearts, One Mind.** *Beast Sense* always prepared; cast once per Long Rest without a slot (no Concentration when cast this way). Spellcasting ability = the ability increased.

---

### Fairy Trickster

*General Feat (Level 4+)*

**+1 Dexterity or Charisma (max 20).**

**Faerie Trod Trotter.** When you take the Disengage action, Difficult Terrain doesn't cost extra movement for the rest of that turn.

**Flustering Strike.** When you hit a creature with an attack roll, it must succeed on a Wis save (DC 8 + relevant ability modifier + PB) or have Disadvantage on saving throws until the end of your next turn. Uses = PB (Long Rest).

---

### Genie Magic

*General Feat (Level 4+)*

**+1 Int, Wis, or Cha (max 20).**

**Wish Magic.** Magic action; cast one level-1 Sorcerer spell (action casting time) from the list. Once per Long Rest. Spellcasting ability = the ability increased. At level 11, spell is cast at level 2; at level 17, at level 3.

---

### Harper Teamwork

*General Feat (Level 4+, requires Harper Agent)*

**+1 Dexterity or Charisma (max 20).**

**Withering Wordplay.** When you take the Help action to assist an ally's attack roll, the enemy also has Disadvantage on its first save before the start of your next turn.

**Inspiring Willpower.** When you succeed on a save to end the Frightened or Paralyzed condition on yourself, one ally within 30 ft. with the same condition also ends that condition.

---

### Lordly Resolve

*General Feat (Level 4+, requires Lords' Alliance Agent)*

**+1 Strength or Charisma (max 20).**

**Standard Bearer.** Bonus Action; choose up to 3 creatures within 60 ft. that can see you. Each can Reaction-end the Prone condition (if Speed > 0) and becomes bolstered: immune to Charmed, Frightened, and possession for 1 minute (or until you're Incapacitated). Advantage on saves vs. those effects if already affected. Once per Long Rest.

### Mythal Touched

*General Feat (Level 4+)*

**+1 Int, Wis, or Cha (max 20).**

**Mythal Ward.** When a spell hits you or you fail a save vs. a spell, take a Reaction and roll 1d20 on the Mythal-Touched Magic table. Uses = PB (Long Rest). Save DC = 8 + chosen modifier + PB.

| 1d20 | Effect |
|---|---|
| 1–2 | You and each creature within 15 ft. make Dex save; take Force damage = 1d8 × spell level (half on success). |
| 3–7 | You and the triggering caster form a telepathic link for 1 hour. |
| 8–10 | Reverse Gravity in a 15-ft radius, 60-ft cylinder centered on you for 1 minute. |
| 11–13 | Both you and the caster make Con save; fail = Stunned until end of their next turn. |
| 14–17 | +2 to AC for 1 minute (may turn spell attack into a miss). |
| 18–19 | Flammable objects within 10 ft. of the caster burst into flame (1d4 Fire, ignites). |
| 20 | Triggering spell dissipates, action/slot not expended. |

### Origin Feats

#### Lesser Dragonmark

*Origin Feat (Prerequisite: Member of appropriate species)*

You possess a Lesser Dragonmark visible somewhere on your body.

**Manifest Dragonmark.** Choose a Mark based on your species (see table below). Choose Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma as your spellcasting ability for the feat's cantrip(s) and spells.

**Guidance of the Mark.** Add **1d4** to any ability check using the skills or tools associated with your Mark.

**Cantrip.** Learn the cantrip(s) associated with your Mark. (Marks of Handling and Warding grant extra spells instead of a cantrip.)

**Spells of the Mark.** Always have the Mark's spells prepared. Cast each once at lowest level without expending a slot (Long Rest recharge). Can cast at higher levels with a spell slot.
