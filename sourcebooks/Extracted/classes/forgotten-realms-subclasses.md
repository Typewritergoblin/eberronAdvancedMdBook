## College of the Moon (Bard)

*Inspire Allies with Primal Tales*

College of the Moon Bards draw on ancient druidic magic of the Moonshae Isles, channeling fey magic and the power of moonwells to support allies and protect nature.

### Level 3: Moon's Inspiration

**Inspired Eclipse.** When you take a Bonus Action to give a creature a Bardic Inspiration die, you can also have the Invisible condition and teleport up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space you can see. Invisibility lasts until the start of your next turn or until you attack, deal damage, or cast a spell.

**Lunar Vitality.** Once per turn when you restore HP to a creature with a spell, you can expend a Bardic Inspiration die and increase the HP restored by a roll of that die. The creature's Speed also increases by 10 ft. until the end of its next turn.

### Level 3: Primal Lore
You learn Druidic and one cantrip from the Druid spell list (counts as a Bard spell; can swap each Bard level). You also gain proficiency in one of: Animal Handling, Insight, Medicine, Nature, Perception, or Survival.

### Level 6: Blessing of Moonlight
*Moonbeam* is always prepared. When you cast *Moonbeam*, you can modify it so that you glow faintly; whenever a creature fails its save against the Moonbeam, another creature of your choice within 60 ft. regains 2d4 HP. Once per Long Rest.

### Level 14: Eventide's Splendor

**Shadow of the New Moon.** When you use Inspired Eclipse, the creature who received the Bardic Inspiration also becomes Invisible and can take a Reaction to teleport up to 30 ft. to a space it can see. Lasts until start of its next turn.

**Vibrance of the Full Moon.** When you use Lunar Vitality, you can roll 1d6 and use that instead of expending a Bardic Inspiration die.

## Knowledge Domain (Cleric)

*Unearth Secrets and Master the Mind*

Clerics of this domain value learning above all. Associated deities in Faerûn include Oghma and Gond.

### Level 3: Blessings of Knowledge
Proficiency with one Artisan's Tool type. Proficiency and Expertise in two skills from: Arcana, History, Nature, or Religion.

### Level 3: Knowledge Domain Spells

| Cleric Level | Prepared Spells |
|---|---|
| 3 | Command, Comprehend Languages\*, Detect Magic\*, Detect Thoughts\*, Identify\*, Mind Spike\* |
| 5 | Dispel Magic, Nondetection, Tongues\* |
| 7 | Arcane Eye\*, Banishment, Confusion |
| 9 | Legend Lore\*, Scrying\*, Synaptic Static |

*\* = Divination school spell*

### Level 3: Mind Magic
Magic action; expend one Channel Divinity use. Cast one prepared spell from the Knowledge Domain Spells table (Divination school only) without a spell slot or material components.

### Level 6: Unfettered Mind
You gain telepathy out to 60 ft. You can contact a number of creatures simultaneously equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum 1). You also gain proficiency in Intelligence saving throws (or another save proficiency if you already have it).

### Level 17: Divine Foreknowledge
Bonus Action; gain Advantage on all D20 Tests for 1 hour. Once per Long Rest (or expend a level 6+ spell slot to restore).

## Banneret (Fighter)

*Rally Fellow Heroes with Inspiring Leadership*

Bannerets protect the innocent and inspire allies. Common in Cormyr and the Silver Marches.

### Level 3: Knightly Envoy

**Comprehension.** Cast *Comprehend Languages* as a Ritual. Cha spellcasting.

**Polyglot.** Learn one language. On a Long Rest, swap it for a language you've heard, seen signed, or read in the past 24 hours.

**Well Spoken.** Proficiency in one of: Insight, Intimidation, Persuasion, or Performance.

### Level 3: Group Recovery
When you use Second Wind to regain HP, choose up to a number of allies within a 30-ft Emanation equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum 1). Each ally regains HP = 1d4 + your Fighter level. Once per Short or Long Rest.

### Level 7: Team Tactics
When you use Group Recovery, each chosen ally has Advantage on D20 Tests until the start of your next turn.

### Level 10: Rallying Surge
When you use Action Surge, choose allies within a 30-ft Emanation (up to your Cha modifier, min. 1). Each can take an immediate Reaction to either:
- **Attack:** Make one attack with a weapon or Unarmed Strike.
- **Move:** Move up to half Speed (no Opportunity Attacks).

### Level 15: Shared Resilience
When an ally within 60 ft. fails a saving throw, you can take a Reaction to expend a use of Indomitable. The ally rerolls with a bonus = your Fighter level; they must use the new roll.

### Level 18: Inspiring Commander

**Bolstered Rally.** Group Recovery and Rallying Surge now affect a 60-ft Emanation (up from 30 ft.).

**Unshakable Bravery.** Immunity to the Charmed and Frightened conditions.

## Oath of the Noble Genies (Paladin)

*Brandish the Elemental Splendor of Genies*

Paladins sworn to this Oath channel the forces of the four Elemental Planes. Many hail from Calimshan.

**Tenets:** Sow creation amid destruction. Lead with splendor and grace. Respect the elements, and fear their wrath.

### Level 3: Elemental Smite
Immediately after you cast *Divine Smite*, you can expend one Channel Divinity use to invoke one effect:

**Dao's Crush.** Target of the smite has the Grappled condition (escape DC = your spell save DC). While Grappled, the target is also Restrained.

**Djinni's Escape.** Teleport up to 30 ft. to a space you can see. Gain semi-incorporeal form until end of your next turn: Resistance to Bludgeoning/Piercing/Slashing; Immunity to Grappled, Prone, and Restrained.

**Efreeti's Fury.** Target takes an extra 2d4 Fire damage; fire jumps to another creature within 30 ft. (also takes 2d4 Fire).

**Marid's Surge.** The smite target and all chosen creatures in a 10-ft Emanation from you make Str save (DC = spell save DC). On fail: pushed 15 ft. and Prone.

### Level 3: Genie Spells

| Paladin Level | Spells |
|---|---|
| 3 | Chromatic Orb, Elementalism, Thunderous Smite |
| 5 | Mirror Image, Phantasmal Force |
| 9 | Fly, Gaseous Form |
| 13 | Conjure Minor Elementals, Summon Elemental |
| 17 | Banishing Smite, Contact Other Plane |

### Level 3: Genie's Splendor
When not wearing armor: AC = 10 + Dex + Cha modifiers (can use Shield). Also gain proficiency in one of: Acrobatics, Intimidation, Performance, or Persuasion.

### Level 7: Aura of Elemental Shielding
Choose a damage type: Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder. You and allies in your Aura of Protection gain Resistance to that type. At the start of each of your turns, you can change the type (no action).

### Level 15: Elemental Rebuke
When hit by an attack, take a Reaction to halve the damage (round down) and force the attacker to make a Dex save (DC = spell save DC). On fail: attacker takes 2d10 + Cha modifier of Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder damage (your choice). On success: half damage. Uses = Cha modifier (Long Rest).

### Level 20: Noble Scion
Bonus Action; activate for 10 minutes (or dismiss). Uses = 1/Long Rest (or expend L5 slot to restore).

**Flight.** Fly Speed 60 ft., hover.

**Minor Wish.** When you or an ally in your Aura of Protection fails a D20 Test, take a Reaction to make the creature succeed instead.

## Winter Walker (Ranger)

*Withstand the Horrors of Frigid Wastelands*

Winter Walkers hone their craft in arctic wastelands like Icewind Dale, becoming frigid terrors.

### Level 3: Frigid Explorer

**Biting Cold.** Damage from your weapon attacks, Ranger spells, and Ranger features ignores Resistance to Cold damage.

**Frost Resistance.** Resistance to Cold damage.

**Polar Strikes.** On a weapon attack hit, deal an extra 1d4 Cold damage (target only once per turn). At Ranger level 11, increases to 1d6.

### Level 3: Hunter's Rime
When you cast *Hunter's Mark*, gain Temporary HP equal to 1d10 + your Ranger level. While a creature is marked by your Hunter's Mark, it can't take the Disengage action.

### Level 3: Winter Walker Spells

| Ranger Level | Spell |
|---|---|
| 3 | Ice Knife |
| 5 | Hold Person |
| 9 | Remove Curse |
| 13 | Ice Storm |
| 17 | Cone of Cold |

### Level 7: Fortifying Soul
Magic Action; choose a number of creatures you can see equal to your Wisdom modifier (min. 1). Each regains HP = 1d10 + your Ranger level and has Advantage on saves vs. Frightened for 1 hour. Once per Long Rest.

### Level 11: Chilling Retribution
When a creature hits you with an attack, take a Reaction to force a Wis save (DC = spell save DC). On fail: Stunned until end of your next turn and Speed reduced to 0. Uses = Wis modifier (Long Rest).

### Level 15: Frozen Haunt
When you cast *Hunter's Mark*, you can adopt a ghostly snowy form (lasts while the spell lasts). Once per Long Rest (or expend a level 4+ slot). Benefits:

**Frozen Soul.** Immunity to Cold damage. When you first adopt this form and at the start of each subsequent turn, each creature of your choice in a 15-ft Emanation takes 2d4 Cold damage.

**Partially Incorporeal.** Immunity to Grappled, Prone, and Restrained. You can move through creatures and objects as Difficult Terrain (take 1d10 Force if you end your turn inside one). If the form ends inside an object, you're shunted to the nearest unoccupied space.

## Scion of the Three (Rogue)

*Become a Gruesome Agent of Malice*

Scions draw power from the Dead Three (Bane, Bhaal, Myrkul), channeling malevolent gifts. Common in Baldur's Gate.

### Level 3: Bloodthirst
When an enemy you can see within 30 ft. takes damage and is Bloodied (not killed), take a Reaction to teleport to an unoccupied space within 5 ft. of them and make one melee attack. Uses = Int modifier (Long Rest).

### Level 3: Dread Allegiance
Choose one: Bane, Bhaal, or Myrkul. Gain a damage Resistance and a cantrip (Int spellcasting). On a Long Rest, you can change your choice.

| God | Damage Resistance | Cantrip |
|---|---|---|
| Bane | Psychic | Minor Illusion |
| Bhaal | Poison | Blade Ward |
| Myrkul | Necrotic | Chill Touch |

### Level 9: Strike Fear
New Cunning Strike option:

**Terrify (Cost: 1d6).** Target makes a Wis save or becomes Frightened for 1 minute (you have Advantage on attacks vs. them while Frightened). Repeats save at end of each of its turns.

### Level 13: Aura of Malevolence
When you use Bloodthirst and teleport, each creature of your choice within 10 ft. of either your departure or destination space takes damage = your Int modifier (same type as your Dread Allegiance Resistance). Ignores Resistance.

### Level 17: Dread Incarnate

**Cutthroat.** Regain one expended Bloodthirst use on a Short Rest.

**Murderous Intent.** When you roll Sneak Attack damage, treat any roll of 1 or 2 as a 3.

## Spellfire Sorcery (Sorcerer)

*Wield Raw Magic*

Your power stems from the Weave itself, manifesting as Spellfire — radiant raw magic that heals allies and sears enemies.

### Level 3: Spellfire Burst
Once per turn, when you spend at least 1 Sorcery Point as part of a Magic action or Bonus Action, you can create one effect:

**Bolstering Flames.** You or one creature within 30 ft. gains Temporary HP = 1d4 + Cha modifier.

**Radiant Fire.** One creature within 30 ft. takes 1d4 Fire or Radiant damage (your choice).

### Level 3: Spellfire Spells

| Sorcerer Level | Spells |
|---|---|
| 3 | Cure Wounds, Guiding Bolt, Lesser Restoration, Scorching Ray |
| 5 | Aura of Vitality, Dispel Magic |
| 7 | Fire Shield, Wall of Fire |
| 9 | Greater Restoration, Flame Strike |

### Level 6: Absorb Spells
*Counterspell* is always prepared. Whenever a target fails its save against a *Counterspell* you cast, regain 1d4 Sorcery Points.

### Level 14: Honed Spellfire
Spellfire Burst improves: add your Sorcerer level to the THP gained from Bolstering Flames; Radiant Fire damage increases to 1d8.

### Level 18: Crown of Spellfire
When you use Innate Sorcery, you can alter it to infuse yourself with Spellfire (once per Long Rest; or spend 5 Sorcery Points to restore). While active:

**Burning Life Force.** Once per turn when hit by an attack, expend up to Cha modifier Hit Point Dice (min. 1). Roll them; reduce damage by the total.

**Flight.** Fly Speed 60 ft., hover.

**Spell Avoidance.** If a spell allows a save for half damage: success = no damage; failure = half damage.

## Bladesinger (Wizard)

*Wield Weapon and Wizardry in Elegant Tandem*

Bladesingers combine swordplay and dance into a graceful combat style. Most hail from ancient elven traditions.

### Level 3: Bladesong
Bonus Action; invoke Bladesong (1 minute, ends early if Incapacitated, armor worn, shield used, or two-handed attack). Uses = Int modifier (Long Rest; also recover one use via Arcane Recovery).

While Bladesong is active:

**Agility.** +Int modifier to AC (min. +1); +10 ft. Speed; Advantage on Dex (Acrobatics).

**Bladework.** Can use Int modifier for attack and damage rolls of proficient weapons.

**Focus.** Add Int modifier to Constitution saves for Concentration.

### Level 3: Training in War and Song
Proficiency with all Melee Martial weapons that lack Two-Handed or Heavy. Use a Melee weapon as a Wizard Spellcasting Focus. Gain proficiency in one of: Acrobatics, Athletics, Performance, or Persuasion.

### Level 6: Extra Attack
Attack twice when you take the Attack action. You can replace one attack with a Wizard cantrip (casting time = action).

### Level 10: Song of Defense
When you take damage while Bladesong is active, take a Reaction to expend a spell slot and reduce the damage by 5 × the slot level.

### Level 14: Song of Victory
After casting a spell with an action casting time, make one weapon attack as a Bonus Action.
