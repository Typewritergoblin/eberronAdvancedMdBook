## Class Features

### Spellcasting (Level 1)
- **Tools Required:** Cast Artificer spells using Thieves' Tools, Tinker's Tools, or any Artisan's Tools as a spellcasting focus.
- **Cantrips:** Know 2 at L1; gain more as shown in table.
- **Spell Slots:** Half-caster (slots shown in table). Intelligence is your spellcasting ability.
- **Preparing Spells:** Prepare a number of spells = Intelligence modifier + half Artificer level (round up).
- **Ritual Casting:** Can cast Artificer spells with Ritual tag as a Ritual.

### Tinker's Magic (Level 1)
You know the *Mending* cantrip. Additionally, you can use Tinker's Tools to create tiny clockwork devices. An item requires 1 hour and 10 gp in materials. Possible items (DM's discretion, maximum 3 simultaneous):
- **Clockwork Toy:** Crawls; makes sounds; distracting.
- **Fire Starter:** Produces small flame; can light candles, torches, etc.
- **Music Box:** Plays a short melody when opened.

### Magic Item Tinker (Level 6)
You can use your Tinker's Tools to modify magic items in three ways. Each item can only be modified once.
- **Charge:** Spend 1 hour; a charged item gains a pool of charges (varies by item type). The charges replenish on a Long Rest.
- **Drain:** Spend 10 minutes; drain an item's remaining charges. Gain Sp equal to charges drained (minimum 1). The item's magic returns on a Long Rest.
- **Transmute:** Spend 1 hour; change the visible appearance of a magic item without altering its properties.

### Flash of Genius (Level 7)
When you or a creature you can see within 30 feet fails an ability check or saving throw, you can take a Reaction to add your Intelligence modifier to the roll, potentially turning a failure into a success. Uses = Intelligence modifier (Long Rest).

### Magic Item Adept (Level 10)
You can attune to up to 4 magic items at once (normal maximum is 3).

### Spell-Storing Item (Level 11)
At the end of a Long Rest, you can touch one simple or martial weapon or a Spellcasting Focus and store a prepared level 1–3 Artificer spell in it. A creature holding the item can use an action to cast the spell (using your spell save DC). The item can hold a number of uses equal to 2 × your Intelligence modifier (minimum 2). The stored spell vanishes when you use this feature again or on your next Long Rest.

### Advanced Artifice (Level 14)
- **Extended Attunement:** You can attune to up to 5 magic items.
- **Refreshed Genius:** You regain one Flash of Genius use when you finish a Short Rest.

### Magic Item Master (Level 18)
You can attune to up to 6 magic items at once.

### Epic Boon (Level 19)
You gain an Epic Boon feat, or another feat for which you qualify.

### Soul of Artifice (Level 20)
- **Cheat Death:** When you are reduced to 0 HP, you can use your Reaction to disintegrate one of your attuned magic items (ending attunement), and drop to 1 HP instead. Once per Long Rest.
- **Magical Guidance:** When you fail an ability check, you can expend a spell slot to add 1d4 per slot level to the roll, potentially turning it into a success.

## Artificer Subclasses

### Alchemist

*Brew Explosive Potions and Alchemical Concoctions*

**Bonus Proficiencies (Level 3):** Alchemist's Supplies and Herbalism Kit; crafting time for potions is halved.

**Alchemist Subclass Spells:**

| Artificer Level | Spells Always Prepared |
|---|---|
| 3 | Healing Word, Ray of Sickness |
| 5 | Flaming Sphere, Melf's Acid Arrow |
| 9 | Gaseous Form, Mass Healing Word |
| 13 | Blight, Death Ward |
| 17 | Cloudkill, Raise Dead |

#### Level 3: Experimental Elixir
At the end of a Long Rest, create a number of Experimental Elixirs equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum 1). On your turn (Magic action), you can also create one by expending a spell slot (level doesn't matter). Roll 1d6 on the table: 
1. **Healing** – Regain 2d4 + Int HP when drunk.
2. **Swiftness** – Speed increases by 10 ft. for 1 hour.
3. **Resilience** – Gain +1 bonus to AC for 10 minutes.
4. **Boldness** – Advantage on attack rolls and saving throws for 1 minute.
5. **Flight** – Fly Speed equal to walking Speed for 10 minutes.
6. **Choose** – You choose which elixir effect from 1–5.

Anyone can drink the elixir or administer it as a Bonus Action. Elixirs last until your next Long Rest unless consumed.

#### Level 5: Alchemical Savant
When you cast a spell using Alchemist's Supplies as its spellcasting focus that restores HP or deals Acid, Fire, Necrotic, or Poison damage, add your Intelligence modifier to one roll of that spell.

#### Level 9: Restorative Reagents
- You always have *Lesser Restoration* prepared.
- When you create an Experimental Elixir, you can also create *Lesser Restoration* as a potion (same rules as elixirs).
- Whenever a creature drinks one of your Experimental Elixirs, it gains Temporary HP equal to 2d6 + your Intelligence modifier.

#### Level 15: Chemical Mastery
- **Alchemical Eruption:** After you cast a spell using Alchemist's Supplies that deals damage, deal extra 2d8 Force damage to one target of the spell.
- **Resistances:** You gain Resistance to Acid and Poison damage, and Immunity to the Poisoned condition.
- **Conjured Cauldron:** You always have *Cloudkill* prepared; cast it once per Long Rest without expending a slot.

### Armorer

*Craft and Don Magically Enhanced Armor*

**Bonus Proficiencies (Level 3):** Heavy armor training and Smith's Tools; crafting time for armor is halved.

**Armorer Subclass Spells:**

| Artificer Level | Spells Always Prepared |
|---|---|
| 3 | Magic Missile, Thunderwave |
| 5 | Mirror Image, Shatter |
| 9 | Hypnotic Pattern, Lightning Bolt |
| 13 | Fire Shield, Greater Invisibility |
| 17 | Passwall, Wall of Force |

#### Level 3: Arcane Armor
As a Magic action (with Smith's Tools), infuse armor you are wearing into Arcane Armor. Benefits:
- The armor's Strength requirement is negated.
- You can use the armor as a spellcasting focus.
- The armor attaches to you; only you can remove it.
- The armor can be donned/doffed as an Action.

If you die, the armor can be removed normally.

#### Level 3: Armor Model
Choose a model for your Arcane Armor (Dreadnaught, Guardian, or Infiltrator). You can change the model on a Short or Long Rest (requires Smith's Tools).

- **Dreadnaught Model:**
  - Gain Skill proficiency in Intimidation or its equivalent in your Intimidation checks.
  - Defensive Field: Bonus Action, gain Temporary HP = your Artificer level. Once per Long Rest (can reuse with spell slot).
  - Helmet: Advantage on saves vs. being Blinded, Deafened, or Stunned.
  - Pauldrons: If a creature hits you with an Opportunity Attack, that creature has Disadvantage on all subsequent attack rolls until end of its next turn.

- **Guardian Model:**
  - Thunder Gauntlets: Unarmed strikes with gauntlets deal 1d8 Thunder damage (+Str or Int modifier). Creatures hit have Disadvantage on attack rolls against targets other than you until start of your next turn.
  - Defensive Field: Bonus Action, gain Temporary HP = your Artificer level. Once per Long Rest (can reuse with spell slot).
  - Electrostatic Field: When an ally within 30 ft is hit by an attack, use Reaction to give that ally a bonus to AC = your Intelligence modifier until start of your next turn.

- **Infiltrator Model:**
  - Lightning Launcher: Ranged weaponized arm; 90/300 ft., deals 1d6 Lightning damage (+Int), once per turn +1d6 Lightning on a hit.
  - Powered Steps: Speed increases by 5 ft.
  - Dampening Field: Advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks; armor's Stealth Disadvantage is negated.

#### Level 5: Extra Attack
Attack twice instead of once when you take the Attack action.

#### Level 9: Improved Armorer
- Learn one additional Armor plan.
- Gain one additional magic item that counts against your magic item attunement.
- Gain +1 bonus to all d20 rolls while wearing Arcane Armor.

#### Level 15: Perfected Armor
Improved model abilities:
- *Dreadnaught:* When you use Defensive Field, all creatures within 15 ft. must succeed on a Strength save (DC = your spell save DC) or be pushed 10 ft. away from you.
- *Guardian:* Electrostatic Field now triggers on any damage (not just attack hits) dealt to an ally within 30 ft., as a Reaction.
- *Infiltrator:* Once per turn when Lightning Launcher hits, the target's speed is reduced by 15 ft. until start of your next turn.

### Artillerist

*Create Eldritch Cannons that Blast Enemies*

**Bonus Proficiencies (Level 3):** Martial ranged weapon proficiency and Woodcarver's Tools; crafting time for wands is halved.

**Artillerist Subclass Spells:**

| Artificer Level | Spells Always Prepared |
|---|---|

| 5 | Scorching Ray, Shatter |
| 9 | Fireball, Wind Wall |
| 13 | Ice Storm, Wall of Fire |
| 17 | Cone of Cold, Wall of Force |

#### Level 3: Eldritch Cannon
As a Magic action, create an Eldritch Cannon in an unoccupied space within 5 ft. (Large or smaller, AC 18, HP = 5 × your Artificer level). It lasts 1 hour or until reduced to 0 HP. On your turn, you can command it (no action required) to move and activate. It uses your spell attack bonus. Uses = Intelligence modifier (Long Rest; more uses with spell slots). Cannon options:

- **Flamethrower:** 15-ft Cone, Dex save (DC = spell save DC), 2d8 Fire damage (half on success). Ignites unattended flammable objects.
- **Force Ballista:** Ranged spell attack (120 ft.), 2d8 Force damage; target pushed 5 ft. on hit.
- **Protector:** 10-ft Emanation; all creatures you designate (including the cannon) gain THP = 1d8 + Int modifier.

**Eldritch Cannon Stat Block:**
- Tiny or Small Construct
- AC 18  
- HP 5 × Artificer level  
- Speed 15 ft.  
- Immune to Poison, Psychic  
- Uses your INT modifier for attack rolls (Force Ballista)

#### Level 5: Arcane Firearm
After completing a Long Rest, turn a wand, staff, or rod into your Arcane Firearm. While you hold it, add +1d8 to one damage roll of Artificer spells you cast.

#### Level 9: Explosive Cannon
- Eldritch Cannons now deal an extra 1d8 damage on a hit/fail.
- As a Bonus Action, you can detonate a cannon within 60 ft.; creatures within 20 ft. must succeed on a Dex save (DC = spell save DC) or take 3d8 Force damage (half on success). The cannon is destroyed.

#### Level 15: Fortified Position
- You can have two Eldritch Cannons active simultaneously.
- You and your allies have Half Cover while within 10 ft. of a cannon.

- **Arcane Empowerment:** When you attack with a magic weapon, you can use your Intelligence modifier instead of Strength or Dexterity for the attack and damage rolls.
- **Weapon Knowledge:** Proficiency with all Martial weapons.

#### Level 3: Steel Defender
At the end of a Long Rest, you create a Steel Defender in an unoccupied space you can see. If it dies or is destroyed, you can recreate it at the end of your next Long Rest.

**Steel Defender Stat Block:**
- Medium Construct
- AC 12 + your Intelligence modifier

- **Repair (3/Day):** In response to taking damage, it regains 2d8 + Int HP (Reaction).
- **Deflect Attack (Reaction):** When a creature you can see hits a creature adjacent to the Defender, impose Disadvantage on the attack roll.

#### Level 5: Extra Attack
Attack twice when you take the Attack action. When you take the Attack action, you can substitute one of those attacks to command the Steel Defender to use Force-Empowered Rend.

#### Level 9: Arcane Jolt
When the Steel Defender hits a target or you hit a target with a magic weapon: deal extra 2d6 Force damage to the target OR restore 2d6 HP to a creature within 30 ft. Uses = Intelligence modifier (Long Rest).

#### Level 15: Improved Defender
- Arcane Jolt now deals/restores 4d6.
- When the Steel Defender uses Deflect Attack, it deals 1d4 + your Intelligence modifier Force damage to the attacker.

#### Level 3: Adventurer's Atlas
At the end of a Long Rest, create a map for each creature of your choice (up to 1 + your Intelligence modifier). The map:
- Automatically updates with the ally's position as they move.
- While you hold your map, you can target map-holders with Artificer spells that require a target you can see (regardless of line of sight), provided they are within the spell's range.
- **Awareness:** You add 1d4 to Initiative rolls.
- **Positioning:** You and map-holders have Advantage on Dexterity checks to avoid being surprised.

#### Level 3: Mapping Magic
- **Illuminated Cartography:** Cast *Faerie Fire* a number of times equal to your Intelligence modifier (Long Rest). Affected creatures are highlighted on your map.

#### Level 5: Guided Precision
When you or an ally holding a map makes an attack roll against a creature highlighted by Illuminated Cartography, add your Intelligence modifier to the roll.

#### Level 15: Superior Atlas

- **Unerring Path:** Allies with your map can't become lost by nonmagical means.

## New Spell: Homunculus Servant

*Level 2 Conjuration (Artificer)*

**Casting Time:** 1 hour (Ritual)
**Range:** 10 ft.
**Components:** V, S, M (a gem or crystal worth 100+ gp)
**Duration:** Instantaneous

You create a Homunculus Servant that is bound to you and serves you until it dies. If it dies, you can recreate it by casting this spell again.

**Homunculus Servant Stat Block:**
- Tiny Construct
- AC 13
- HP 5 + 5 × the spell's level
- Speed 20 ft., Fly 40 ft.
- Skills: Perception +4, Stealth +4
- **Evasion:** When it succeeds on a Dex save vs. a half-damage effect, it takes no damage.
- **Channel Magic (Reaction):** When you cast a spell with "touch" range, the Homunculus can deliver the touch for you (must be within 120 ft.).

## Alternate Level 20 Capstones

Source: *Exploring Eberron* (2024), Chapter 6

With DM permission, an Artificer who reaches level 20 can choose an alternative feature associated with their subclass instead of Soul of Artifice.

### Alchemist: Perfected Elixirs

You can create two legendary elixirs per Long Rest:

**Panacea:** Fully heals a creature, cures all conditions, and if the creature dies within 1 hour of drinking it, they are revived (as if by Revivify), provided their body is intact.

### Armorer: Overcharge
