# Frontiers of Eberron — Subclasses

> Source: Frontiers of Eberron: Across the Threshold (Frontiers24CharacterOptions.md)
> Six subclasses for PHB24 classes, themed around the Western Frontier (Breland, Droaam, Eldeen Reaches).

---

## Barbarian: Path of the Demonshard

*Empower yourself with a fiendish presence. A shard of demonglass embeds a fiend's essence into the barbarian's body. Warriors of the Carrion Tribes, champions of Turakbar's Fist, Znir hwyri, and Ghaash'kala warriors all use this power.*

### Level 3: Demontongue

You can speak and understand your choice of either Abyssal or Infernal.

### Level 3: Fiendish Punishment

While your Rage is active, if you take damage from a creature you can see within 60 feet, that creature takes **1d4 Fire or Necrotic damage** (your choice). Once per turn only.

Additionally, if an allied creature within 10 feet of you takes damage from a creature you can see within 60 feet, you can use your **Reaction** to cause the attacker to take 1d4 Fire or Necrotic damage.

| Barbarian Level | Damage |
|---|---|
| 3 | 1d4 |
| 6 | 1d6 |
| 10 | 1d8 |
| 14 | 1d10 |
| 20 | 1d12 |

### Level 6: Just One Master

While your Rage is active:
- **Advantage** on saving throws against Charmed and Frightened conditions.
- **Resistance** to Psychic damage.
- If you take Psychic damage or make a save against Charmed/Frightened: you **must** use your Reaction (if available) to immediately enter a Rage. This Rage use does **not** expend a use of Rage.

### Level 10: Overflowing Malice

When a creature triggers your Fiendish Punishment feature, **all other creatures within 10 feet of you** take the damage too.

### Level 14: Aspect of Tyranny

When a creature takes damage from Fiendish Punishment or Overflowing Malice, you can use your **Reaction** to force one affected creature to make a **Wisdom saving throw** (DC 8 + Constitution modifier + Proficiency Bonus). The creature suffers a **−1 penalty per additional creature** affected on the same turn.

- **Failed save:** 1d10 Necrotic damage + **Frightened** for 1 minute (save each turn to end).
- **Success:** No effect.

## Bard: College of Wands

*Spellcasting as art; every duel is a symphony. Entertainers, Smugglers, and Spies who lay enemies low with a wand and a word.*

### Level 3: Bonus Cantrip

Learn one cantrip from the **Sorcerer spell list** (*Fire Bolt* recommended). It counts as a Bard cantrip but doesn't count against your known cantrips.

You may use a **Wand, Rod, or Orb** as a Spellcasting Focus for Bard spells.

### Level 3: Wandslinger's Presence

After speaking to a creature for at least 1 minute: if that creature rolls Initiative equal to or higher than yours, it must make a **Wisdom saving throw** (your spell save DC). On a failed save, its Initiative drops to equal yours and it acts immediately after you.

### Level 3: At Your Fingertips

When you hit a creature with a Bard cantrip, or when a creature fails a save against one of your Bard cantrips, you can expend one use of **Bardic Inspiration** to deal **extra damage** to that creature. Once per round, on your turn.

| Bard Level | Extra Damage |
|---|---|
| 3 | +2d6 |
| 5 | +3d6 |
| 10 | +5d6 |
| 15 | +8d6 |

### Level 6: Got Your Back

When you use At Your Fingertips, you can use your **Bonus Action** that same turn to grant **Advantage on the next saving throw** (for 1 minute) to one creature you can see within 60 feet.

### Level 14: Ain't Big Enough for the Both of Us

Whenever a use of Bardic Inspiration is expended (by you or an ally adding a die to a D20 Test within 60 feet), one creature of your choice within 60 feet that can see you must make a **Wisdom saving throw** (your spell save DC).

- **Failed save:** **Frightened** until end of its next turn.
- **Already Frightened:** must use its Reaction (if available) to move as far from you as possible via the safest route.

## Cleric: Commerce Domain

*Prayer may be the currency of the gods, but hard cash makes the world go round. Kol Korran (and, for the greedy, the Keeper) are the patron deities.*

### Level 3: Commerce Domain Spells

| Cleric Level | Prepared Spells |
|---|---|
| 3 | *Augury, Charm Person, Comprehend Languages, Locate Object* |
| 5 | *Glyph of Warding, Tongues* |
| 7 | *Divination, Leomund's Secret Chest* |
| 9 | *Legend Lore, Rary's Telepathic Bond* |

### Level 3: Divine Wealth

On each Long Rest, conjure divine coins (number scales with level):

| Cleric Level | Coins per Long Rest |
|---|---|
| 3 | 2 |
| 5 | 3 |
| 9 | 4 |
| 13 | 5 |
| 17 | 6 |

On any saving throw against a magical effect from a hostile creature, choose before rolling:

- **Spend a Coin:** Roll 1d4 and **add** to the save.
- **Gain a Coin:** Roll 1d4 and **subtract** from the save.

### Level 3: Money Spinner

**Magic action** — expend Channel Divinity. Choose a number of creatures within 30 feet equal to *1 + number of divine coins you expend*. For each target, choose Bribe or Reward:

- **Reward:** Wisdom saving throw. Fail = **Charmed** for 1 minute (or until takes damage); the Charmed creature is Friendly to you and designated allies.
- **Bribe:** Wisdom saving throw. Fail = **Blinded** for 1 minute (or until takes damage).

### Level 6: Divine Bargain

**Bonus Action** — conduct a metaphysical trade with one creature within 30 feet. Choose Buy or Sell:

- **Buy:** Give one divine coin to target. Target takes **1d8 Necrotic** damage; you or an ally within 30 feet regains HP equal to damage taken.
- **Sell:** Gain one divine coin. You take **1d8 Necrotic** damage; target regains HP equal to damage taken. (Damage cannot be reduced by any means.)

### Level 17: Open Market

- Use **2d8** instead of 1d8 for Divine Bargain.
- When using Divine Bargain, you may target up to **6 creatures** within 30 feet, buying or selling separately per target using the same 2d8 roll. If you do so, you can't do it again until a Short Rest.

## Ranger: Bloodhound

*Follow your mark to the bitter end. House Tharashk trains bounty hunters in these techniques; Znir hunters and worgs of the Great Pack use them to track enemies of Droaam.*

### Level 3: Bloodhound Spells

| Ranger Level | Prepared Spells |
|---|---|
| 3 | *Alarm* |
| 5 | *Hold Person* |
| 9 | *Clairvoyance* |
| 13 | *Locate Creature* |
| 17 | *Scrying* |

### Level 3: Ley Line Tracker

**Mark Bounty.** At end of each Short or Long Rest, designate one creature as your bounty. You must have seen the creature or possess an object that's been in their possession for 24+ hours. Once per turn, you can change your bounty to one creature you can see (free).

**Masterful Tracking.** Your Divination school Ranger spells can target your bounty at **any distance** and without line of sight, unless they are protected by *Nondetection* or on another plane.

### Level 3: Unwavering Attention

When you hit your **bounty** or a creature currently marked by your *Hunter's Mark*, deal **+1d6 damage** (once per turn). If this extra damage is dealt via an Opportunity Attack, your Reaction resets at the start of the next creature's turn (instead of your own next turn).

### Level 7: Relentless Pursuit

If your bounty or *Hunter's Mark* target ends its turn more than 5 feet away from you and you can see it, use your **Reaction** to move your full speed toward it (no Opportunity Attacks).

### Level 11: Eyes on the Prize

When you take the Attack action and **target only one creature** with all attacks, make one **additional attack** against that creature:

- **Melee:** On hit → target is **Grappled or Prone** (your choice).
- **Ranged:** On hit → target is **Prone**.

### Level 15: Nowhere to Hide

Cast ***True Seeing*** on yourself without expending a spell slot or Material components. Recharges on Short or Long Rest.

Additionally, magic that conceals your bounty does not affect your Ley Line Tracker Divination spell targeting.

## Sorcerer: Nemesis Sorcery

*Devastate foes with speed and precision. You specialize in arcane dueling, absolute precision, and breaking spells. Forged on the battlefields of the Last War.*

**Note:** This subclass's Levels 6/14/18 features appear after the Warlock subclass header in the source document due to layout formatting, but they reference Sorcery Points and belong to this subclass.

### Level 3: Nemesis Spells

| Sorcerer Level | Prepared Spells |
|---|---|
| 3 | *Compelled Duel, Mirror Image, See Invisibility, Shield* |
| 5 | *Counterspell, Slow* |
| 7 | *Fire Shield, Otiluke's Resilient Sphere* |
| 9 | *Circle of Power, Mislead* |

### Level 3: Readied Magic

When you use an Action to **Ready a spell**, you have **Advantage on saving throws and ability checks** until the start of your next turn.

Additionally, if the trigger for your readied spell occurs, you can cast the readied spell **without using your Reaction**.

If the trigger does not resolve, you choose not to act, or you lose Concentration: **gain Sorcery Points equal to the spell's level** at the start of your next turn. You still expend the spell slot normally.

### Level 6: Spell Deflection

When you take damage from a spell or other magical effect, use your **Reaction** to **halve the damage**.

### Level 14: Always Ready

Use a **Bonus Action** to Ready a spell as if you had used your Action. Expending a spell slot when readying this way does **not** count against the one-slot-per-turn rule.

Once used, you can't use it again until a Short or Long Rest, unless you **spend 4 Sorcery Points** to restore the use.

### Level 18: Mindset of Perfect Prediction

**Action:** Activate your Mindset of Perfect Prediction, gaining all benefits below until it ends.

Starting the turn after activation, make a **Charisma saving throw** at the end of each of your turns (DC 8 + Charisma modifier + Proficiency Bonus). On a failed save, the Mindset ends. If concentrating on multiple spells when it ends, choose one to maintain; the rest end. Once used, can't activate again until Long Rest.

- **Quickened Mind.** When rolling Initiative, you may instead choose Initiative count **31**.
- **Expansive Mind.** You can **concentrate on any number of spells simultaneously**. On a failed Concentration save, all Concentrated spells end.
- **Predictive Mind.** *Counterspell* is always prepared. You can cast it as a **6th-level spell without expending a spell slot**.

## Warlock: Stone Sovereign Patron

*Hear the whispering of the stones. The daelkyr Orlassk, fey King Grayfinger, medusa Queen Sheshka, earth elementals of Lorghalan gnomes — all grant this power.*

### Level 3: Eternity in Stone

When you reduce a creature to 0 Hit Points, you can cause it to **turn to stone** (gains **Petrified** condition). If you do, gain **Temporary Hit Points = 1d10 + your Warlock level**.

Once used, recharges on Short or Long Rest.

### Level 3: Stone Speaker

**Magic action** — imbue stones in a 30-ft Emanation with limited sentience. Question stones about events in the past day within the area (creatures that passed, items removed, etc.).

If an earth Elemental or Petrified creature is in the area, you can communicate with it as though you shared a language (no magical influence granted).

Once used, recharges on Long Rest.

### Level 3: Stone Sovereign Spells

| Warlock Level | Prepared Spells |
|---|---|
| 3 | *Absorb Elements, Earthbind (XGE), Earth Tremor (XGE), Spike Growth* |
| 5 | *Magic Circle, Slow* |
| 7 | *Stone Shape, Stoneskin* |
| 9 | *Bigby's Hand, Wall of Stone* |

### Level 6: Form of Stone

**Immunity improvement:** While you have the Petrified condition (from any source), you are **aware of surroundings** and have **Immunity to Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing damage**.

**Reaction:** When you take damage, give yourself the Petrified condition until the start of your next turn. Petrified condition applies *before* the triggering damage (potentially granting Resistance or Immunity). When condition ends, you can choose to **emerge from an adjacent stone surface** into any unoccupied space within 30 feet.

Once used, recharges on Short or Long Rest.

### Level 10: Stone's Embrace

*Meld into Stone* is always prepared. When you cast it, up to **5 willing creatures** within 5 feet (each touching a stone object large enough to contain them) can also gain the benefits of the spell.

Creatures who finish a Short or Long Rest while under *Meld into Stone* via this feature **gain Resistance to the first instance of damage** they take after leaving the stone.

### Level 14: Stone Servant

Touch one creature with the **Petrified** condition. It becomes an **Earth Elemental Spirit** under your control, as if you cast *Summon Elemental* at 6th level. Creature size doesn't change. **No Concentration required.**

Duration: until *Greater Restoration* is cast on it, until the Elemental Spirit is reduced to 0 HP, or until you use this feature again.
