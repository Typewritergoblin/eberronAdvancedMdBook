### Tempest Cleric
New Spells Always Prepared:
Tempest Domain Spells:
| Cleric Level  | Prepared Spells |
| 3 | Ice Knife, Thunderwave, Misty Step, Gust of Wind |
| 5 | Lightning Bolt, Call Lightning |
| 7 | Ice Storm, Control Water |
| 9 | Cone of Cold, Storm of Radiance  | 
New Lvl 3 Ability:
Stormbringer - When dealing radiant, fire, ice, or force damage, you can instead deal lightning or thunder damage. 
Wrath of the Storm - You can thunderously rebuke attackers. When a creature within 5 feet of you that you can see hits you with an attack, you can use your reaction to cause the creature to make a Dexterity saving throw. The creature takes 2d8 lightning or thunder damage (your choice) on a failed saving throw, and half as much damage on a successful one. Vou can use this feature a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier (a minimum of once). Regain all expended uses on a long rest.
Channel Divinity - Destructive Wrath - You can use your Channel Divinity to wield the power of the storm with unchecked ferocity. When you roll lightning or thunder damage, you can use your Channel Divinity to have a number of dice equal to 1 + your proficiency modifier automatically deal maximum damage. Remaining dice must be rolled normally.


- 	Level 6 
Thunderbolt Strike -  At 6th leveI, when you deal lightning damage to a Large or smaller creature, you can also push it up to 10 feet away from you.
Level 8
Divine Strike - At 8th level, you gain the ability to infuse your weapon strikes with divine energy. Once on each of your turns when you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can cause the attack to deal an extra ld8 thunder damage to the target. When you reach 14th level, the extra damage increases to 2d8.
Stormborn - At 17th level, you have a flying speed equal to your current walking speed whenever you are not underground or indoors.
