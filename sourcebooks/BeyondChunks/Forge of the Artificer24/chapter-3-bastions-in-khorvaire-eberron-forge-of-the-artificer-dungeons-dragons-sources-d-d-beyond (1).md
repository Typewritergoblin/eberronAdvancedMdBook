The [Dungeon Master’s Guide](/sources/dnd/dmg-2024) contains the rules for [Bastions](/sources/dnd/dmg-2024/bastions), which allow characters to establish a base of operations for their adventures. This chapter adds further options for characters to establish Bastions that use Eberron’s special qualities and resources.

## Mobile Bastions

The [Bastion rules](/sources/dnd/dmg-2024/bastions) in the [Dungeon Master’s Guide](/sources/dnd/dmg-2024) assume that characters establish their Bastions in a fixed location and that their adventures might take them away from their Bastions at times, perhaps even for long periods. But in Eberron (and perhaps other settings), characters might establish a permanent base of operations that isn’t linked to a fixed location—maybe on a sailing ship, a lightning rail cart, or even an airship. The DM decides whether to allow characters to establish a mobile Bastion, which uses the following alterations to the normal Bastion rules.

A mobile Bastion has two basic requirements: it must be built in a vehicle form, and one of its special facilities must allow for propulsion. (The [Lyrandar Helm](#LyrandarHelm), [Navigator’s Helm](#NavigatorsHelm), and [Orien Helm](#OrienHelm) described in this chapter fulfill that requirement.) If multiple characters combine their Bastions into a single vehicle, each Bastion must include the same kind of special facility that allows for propulsion, and in order for the vehicle to travel, the characters must all issue the Empower order to those facilities at the same time. In this case, however, the combined Bastion can travel more than 8 hours per day; the facility hirelings can operate the vehicle in shifts. Thus, a combined Bastion with at least three Navigator’s Helm facilities can sail for 24 hours a day as the three hirelings trade off navigation in 8-hour shifts.

If characters are traveling with their mobile Bastions, they can also have encounters and face obstacles just as if they were traveling away from their Bastions. When the DM is planning [obstacles for such a journey](/sources/dnd/dmg-2024/running-the-game#JourneyStageChallenges) (as described in the [Dungeon Master’s Guide](/sources/dnd/dmg-2024)), a potential consequence of an encounter or obstacle is the temporary loss of access to one or more of the Bastion’s facilities, including the facility that allows propulsion.

Players and the DM should work together to create a [Bastion map](/sources/dnd/dmg-2024/bastions#BastionMap) that fits within the chosen vehicle. Facilities can be slightly larger or smaller than the rules in the [Dungeon Master’s Guide](/sources/dnd/dmg-2024) specify, at the DM’s discretion, in order to fit. A mobile Bastion can’t have defensive walls.

### Artificer’s Forge

*Level 13 Bastion Facility*

**Prerequisite:** Ability to use Artisan’s Tools as a [Spellcasting Focus](/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#SpellcastingFocus "//www.dndbeyond.com/rules-glossary/108-tooltip?disable-webm=1&disable-webm=1")

**Space:** Roomy

**Hirelings:** 2

**Order:** Craft

An Artificer’s Forge includes two magewright hirelings who are skilled in creating magic items.

***Crafting Assistants.*** If you [create a magic item](/sources/dnd/dmg-2024/dms-toolbox#CreatingaMagicItem) in your Bastion using the rules in the [Dungeon Master’s Guide](/sources/dnd/dmg-2024), one or both of this facility’s hirelings can assist you, reducing the time required for the crafting.

***Craft: Magic Item.*** When you issue the Craft order to this facility, you commission the facility’s hirelings to craft a [Common or an Uncommon magic item](/sources/dnd/dmg-2024/treasure#MagicItems) chosen by you from the [Dungeon Master’s Guide](/sources/dnd/dmg-2024). The facility has the tool required to craft the item, and the hirelings have proficiency with that tool as well as proficiency in the [Arcana](/sources/dnd/free-rules/playing-the-game#Skills "//www.dndbeyond.com/skills/6-tooltip?disable-webm=1&disable-webm=1") skill. The two hirelings assist each other, reducing the time required to craft the item. If the item allows its user to cast any spells from it, the hirelings have those spells prepared.

***Charge Refreshment.*** Whenever a magic item in your Bastion regains charges, it regains 1 extra expended charge.

### Construct Forge

*Level 17 Bastion Facility*

**Prerequisite:** Ability to use Artisan’s Tools as a [Spellcasting Focus](/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#SpellcastingFocus "//www.dndbeyond.com/rules-glossary/108-tooltip?disable-webm=1&disable-webm=1")

**Space:** Vast

**Hirelings:** 2

**Order:** Recruit

A Construct Forge is used to create Constructs.

***Recruit: Construct.*** When you issue the Recruit order to this facility, you commission the facility’s hirelings to craft a Construct from the Forge Constructs table. The construction takes the time and costs you the amount listed in the table.

Constructs made in this facility count as Bastion Defenders. Deduct any you lose from your Bastion Defenders roster. You can choose not to count one or more of these creatures as Bastion Defenders, in which case they can’t be called on to defend the Bastion. Instead, they act in accordance with their nature and use their stat blocks in the [*Monster Manual*](/sources/dnd/mm-2024).

### Dragonmark Outpost

*Level 5 Bastion Facility*

**Prerequisite:** Renown 10+ with any [dragonmarked house](/sources/dnd/efota/character-options#Dragonmarks)

**Space:** Roomy

**Hirelings:** 1

**Order:** Empower

A Dragonmark Outpost is an area set aside for the use of your dragonmarked house. The hireling is a house agent and serves as a liaison between you and your superiors in the house.

When you add this facility to your Bastion, your Renown Score with your house increases by 1.

***Dragonmark Empowerment (Requires Any Dragonmark Feat).*** When you finish a Long Rest in your Bastion, you gain one extra spell slot of level 2. You can use this spell slot to cast only a spell you have prepared because of a [Dragonmark feat](/sources/dnd/efota/character-options#DragonmarkFeats).

***Empower: House Favor.*** When you issue the Empower order to this facility, you instruct your hireling to call in a favor from your dragonmarked house. You can use any benefit of your house that your Renown Score affords you (as described in [chapter 5](/sources/dnd/efota/dragonmarked-intrigue#DragonmarkedHouseRenown)), and this favor doesn’t count against the total number of favors available to you. Once you use this feature, you can’t use it again until your Renown Score with your house increases.

### Inquisitive Agency

*Level 13 Bastion Facility*

**Prerequisite:** None

**Space:** Roomy

**Hirelings:** 1

**Order:** Research

This facility is an office for an inquisitive agency—either the headquarters of an agency you control or a branch office for a larger agency (such as [Information Acquisition](/sources/dnd/efota/sharn-inquisitives#MiddleDura) or [Karr’Aashta’s Investigations](/sources/dnd/efota/sharn-inquisitives#UpperTavicksLanding), both of which are discussed in chapter 4). Its one hireling is a secretary who maintains an extensive directory of useful contacts and helpful agents.

***Research: Information Gathering.*** When you issue the Research order to this facility, you commission the agency’s secretary to gather information from contacts and agents regarding a specific individual you designate, assembling a comprehensive picture of that person’s activities during the previous 7 days and over the course of the next 7 days, within 10 miles of your Bastion. Unless the DM deems that certain information is beyond these agents’ ability to uncover, you learn the following information:

-   Where the person is and has been staying
-   What they’ve spent money on
-   Whom they’ve met with
-   Where they’ve shown identification papers to the authorities

If your Bastion is mobile, it takes time to establish this network of agents in a new place. When your Bastion is traveling, you can’t issue the Research order to this facility. After it arrives at its destination, the first time you issue the Research order, this facility’s hireling spends 7 days establishing a new network, after which you can use the Research order normally.

### Kundarak Vault

*Level 9 Bastion Facility*

**Prerequisite:** Renown 15+ with [House Kundarak](/sources/dnd/efota/dragonmarked-intrigue#HouseKundarak)

**Space:** Cramped

**Hirelings:** 1

**Order:** Trade

A Kundarak Vault occupies space in your Bastion like any other special facility, but it is an extradimensional space that can be accessed from other places. As a Kundarak facility, it provides exceptionally secure storage for your trade goods and other treasures. Only you and your Bastion’s hirelings can open the vault door.

***Kundarak Access.*** Because the vault is an extradimensional space, you (and only you) can access its contents from any Kundarak bank facility in the world. These banks are found in cities across [Khorvaire](/sources/dnd/erftlw/khorvaire-gazeteer#NationsofKhorvaire) (and also in [Stormreach, in Xen’drik](/sources/dnd/erftlw/khorvaire-gazeteer-distant-lands#XendrikNotableLocations)).

***Trade: Goods.*** When you issue the Trade order to this facility, its hireling spends the next 7 days procuring nonmagical items that have a total value of 2,000 GP or less and stores them in the Kundarak Vault, or the hireling uses those 7 days to sell goods in the Kundarak Vault. You bear the total cost of any purchases, and the maximum value of the items purchased increases to 5,000 GP when you reach level 13.

When you sell goods from your Kundarak Vault, the buyer pays you 20 percent more than the standard price; this profit increases to 50 percent when you reach level 13 and 100 percent when you reach level 17.

### Lyrandar Helm

*Level 13 Bastion Facility*

**Prerequisite:** Renown 25+ with [House Lyrandar](/sources/dnd/efota/dragonmarked-intrigue#HouseLyrandar)

**Space:** Cramped

**Hirelings:** 1

**Order:** Empower

A Lyrandar Helm allows a mobile Bastion built within an elemental airship or an elemental galleon to travel. This facility’s hireling is a [dragonmarked heir of House Lyrandar](/sources/dnd/efota/character-options#HouseLyrandarHeir). On an elemental airship, the airship’s Helm crew station is part of this facility (see [chapter 7](/sources/dnd/efota/elemental-airships#CrewMembersandStations)).

***Empower: Travel.*** When you issue the Empower order to this facility, you instruct its hireling to pilot the Bastion to a destination you specify. The Bastion travels at the vehicle’s speed, 8 hours per day, for the duration of the Bastion turn. The Bastion stops early when it reaches the specified destination or if something happens to stop your travel.

When you issue the Empower order to this facility, the DM rolls on the [Bastion Events table](/sources/dnd/dmg-2024/bastions#BastionEvents) as if you had issued the Maintain order to your Bastion.

### Manifest Zone

*Level 13 Bastion Facility*

**Prerequisite:** Ability to use a [Spellcasting Focus](/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#SpellcastingFocus "//www.dndbeyond.com/rules-glossary/108-tooltip?disable-webm=1&disable-webm=1")

**Space:** Vast

**Hirelings:** 1

**Order:** Empower

A Bastion with a Manifest Zone is infused with the planar energy of one of Eberron’s planes of existence. Choose the plane when you build this facility.

***Empower: Manifest Charm.*** When you issue the Empower order to this facility, the facility’s hireling conducts magical rites for the next 7 days. At the end of that period, you gain a special Charm (see “[Supernatural Gifts](/sources/dnd/dmg-2024/dms-toolbox#SupernaturalGifts)” in the [Dungeon Master’s Guide](/sources/dnd/dmg-2024)) called a Manifest Charm, which lasts until you use it. You don’t need to be in the Bastion while the rites are performed to gain their benefit. The effects of the Charm depend on the facility’s linked plane:

-   **Daanvi, the Perfect Order.** You can expend the Charm (no action required) to end the [Blinded](/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#BlindedCondition "//www.dndbeyond.com/conditions/1-tooltip?disable-webm=1&disable-webm=1"), [Deafened](/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#DeafenedCondition "//www.dndbeyond.com/conditions/3-tooltip?disable-webm=1&disable-webm=1"), [Paralyzed](/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#ParalyzedCondition "//www.dndbeyond.com/conditions/9-tooltip?disable-webm=1&disable-webm=1"), or [Poisoned](/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#PoisonedCondition "//www.dndbeyond.com/conditions/11-tooltip?disable-webm=1&disable-webm=1") condition on yourself.
    
-   **Dolurrh, the Realm of the Dead.** When you use a spell slot to cast a spell from the Necromancy school, you can expend the Charm (no action required) to treat the spell as if it were cast using a spell slot 1 level above the slot expended.
    
-   **Fernia, the Sea of Fire.** When you use a spell slot to cast a spell that deals Fire damage, you can expend the Charm (no action required) to treat the spell as if it were cast using a spell slot 1 level above the slot expended.
    
-   **Irian, the Eternal Dawn.** When you use a spell slot to cast a spell that restores Hit Points or deals Radiant damage, you can expend the Charm (no action required) to treat the spell as if it were cast using a spell slot 1 level above the slot expended.
    
-   **Lamannia, the Twilight Forest.** When you use a spell slot to cast a spell that conjures or summons a Beast, an Elemental, or a Fey (including [Conjure Animals](/spells/2619044-conjure-animals "//www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619044-tooltip?disable-webm=1&disable-webm=1"), [Conjure Elemental](/spells/2619047-conjure-elemental "//www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619047-tooltip?disable-webm=1&disable-webm=1"), [Conjure Fey](/spells/2619048-conjure-fey "//www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619048-tooltip?disable-webm=1&disable-webm=1"), [Conjure Minor Elementals](/spells/2619049-conjure-minor-elementals "//www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619049-tooltip?disable-webm=1&disable-webm=1"), [Conjure Woodland Beings](/spells/2619051-conjure-woodland-beings "//www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619051-tooltip?disable-webm=1&disable-webm=1"), [Find Steed](/spells/2618879-find-steed "//www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2618879-tooltip?disable-webm=1&disable-webm=1"), [Summon Beast](/spells/2619106-summon-beast "//www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619106-tooltip?disable-webm=1&disable-webm=1"), [Summon Elemental](/spells/2619113-summon-elemental "//www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619113-tooltip?disable-webm=1&disable-webm=1"), or [Summon Fey](/spells/2619115-summon-fey "//www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619115-tooltip?disable-webm=1&disable-webm=1") ), you can expend the Charm (no action required) to treat the spell as if it were cast using a spell slot 1 level above the slot expended.
    
-   **Mabar, the Endless Night.** When you use a spell slot to cast a spell that animates or summons an Undead (including [Animate Dead](/spells/2618853-animate-dead "//www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2618853-tooltip?disable-webm=1&disable-webm=1"), [Create Undead](/spells/2619076-create-undead "//www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619076-tooltip?disable-webm=1&disable-webm=1"), or [Summon Undead](/spells/2619119-summon-undead "//www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619119-tooltip?disable-webm=1&disable-webm=1") ), you can expend the Charm (no action required) to treat the spell as if it were cast using a spell slot 1 level above the slot expended.
    
-   **Risia, the Plain of Ice.** When you use a spell slot to cast a spell that deals Cold damage, you can expend the Charm (no action required) to treat the spell as if it were cast using a spell slot 1 level above the slot expended.
    
-   **Shavarath, the Battleground.** When you score a Critical Hit, you can expend the Charm (no action required) to deal an extra 10 damage of one of the types dealt by the attack.
    
-   **Syrania, the Azure Sky.** You can expend the Charm as a Bonus Action to gain a Fly Speed equal to your Speed for 1 minute.
    
-   **Thelanis, the Faerie Court.** When you cast a spell from the Enchantment or Illusion school, you can expend the Charm (no action required) to give one target of the spell Disadvantage on its saving throw against the spell.
    
-   **Xoriat, the Realm of Delirium.** When you use a spell slot to cast a spell, you can expend the Charm (no action required) to roll on the [Wild Magic Surge table](/sources/dnd/phb-2024/character-classes-continued#WildMagicSurge) in the [Player’s Handbook](/sources/dnd/phb-2024).
    

If your Bastion is mobile, its connection to the linked plane of existence is suspended while the Bastion moves, and you can’t issue the Empower order to this facility while it is in transit. When the Bastion reaches a destination, you can issue the Empower order to establish a new planar connection, linking the Bastion either to the same plane of existence or to a different one you choose. Establishing the new connection takes 7 days, after which you can issue the Empower order normally.

### Museum

*Level 13 Bastion Facility*

**Prerequisite:** None

**Space:** Roomy

**Hirelings:** 1

**Order:** Research

A Museum contains relics and treasures brought back from expeditions you participated in or sponsored, carefully cataloged and displayed.

***Research: Specialized Lore.*** When you issue the Research order to this facility, you commission the hireling to study particular treasures in the museum. The work takes 7 days. The hireling gains knowledge regarding some question of ancient history as if they had cast the [Legend Lore](/spells/2619010-legend-lore "//www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619010-tooltip?disable-webm=1&disable-webm=1") spell, then shares this knowledge with you the next time you speak with them.

***Secrets of the Past.*** Your Museum contains one treasure that gives you a magical Charm (see “[Supernatural Gifts](/sources/dnd/dmg-2024/dms-toolbox#SupernaturalGifts)” in the [Dungeon Master’s Guide](/sources/dnd/dmg-2024)) when you spend 7 days studying it and then finish a Long Rest in your Bastion. The Charm lasts for 7 days or until you use it. You can’t gain another Charm from this facility while you still have one. The effect of the Charm depends on the origin of the treasure in your Museum. You can choose one of the following options (your DM might make more options available or limit the existing options based on your past expeditions):

-   ***Charm of Argonnessen.*** While you have this Charm, you know [Draconic](/sources/dnd/phb-2024/creating-a-character#StandardLanguages). You can expend the Charm to cast [Dragon’s Breath](/spells/2619150-dragons-breath "//www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619150-tooltip?disable-webm=1&disable-webm=1") (level 4 version), targeting yourself only.
    
-   ***Charm of Ashtakala.*** While you have this Charm, you know [Abyssal](/sources/dnd/phb-2024/creating-a-character#RareLanguages) and [Infernal](/sources/dnd/phb-2024/creating-a-character#RareLanguages). You can expend the Charm to cast [Ray of Sickness](/spells/2618935-ray-of-sickness "//www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2618935-tooltip?disable-webm=1&disable-webm=1") (level 4 version).
    
-   ***Charm of Dhakaan.*** While you have this Charm, you know the [Goblin language](/sources/dnd/phb-2024/creating-a-character#StandardLanguages). You can expend the Charm to cast [Staggering Smite](/spells/2619084-staggering-smite "//www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619084-tooltip?disable-webm=1&disable-webm=1").
    
-   ***Charm of Khyber.*** While you have this Charm, you have [Darkvision](/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#Darkvision "//www.dndbeyond.com/senses/2-tooltip?disable-webm=1&disable-webm=1") with a range of 120 feet. You can expend the Charm to gain [Truesight](/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#Truesight "//www.dndbeyond.com/senses/4-tooltip?disable-webm=1&disable-webm=1") with a range of 60 feet for 1 minute.
    
-   ***Charm of Xen’drik.*** While you have this Charm, you know the [Giant language](/sources/dnd/phb-2024/creating-a-character#StandardLanguages). As a Bonus Action, you can expend the Charm to change your Strength score to 25 for 1 hour.
    
-   ***Enlarging the Facility.*** You can enlarge your Museum to a Vast facility by spending 2,000 GP. If you do so, the Museum can have two treasures with different origins. A Vast Museum gains two additional hirelings, for a total of three. Assign names and personalities to them as you see fit.

### Orien Helm

**Prerequisite:** Renown 15+ with [House Orien](/sources/dnd/efota/dragonmarked-intrigue#HouseOrien)

**Space:** Cramped

**Hirelings:** 1

**Order:** Empower

An Orien Helm allows a mobile Bastion built within a Lightning Rail cart to travel. This facility’s hireling is a [dragonmarked heir of House Orien](/sources/dnd/efota/character-options#HouseOrienHeir).
