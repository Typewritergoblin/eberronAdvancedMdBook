# Classes

<!-- toc -->

Adventurers in Khorvaire are defined as much by what they *do* as by where they came from. A character's class is their vocation — the trained discipline, innate power, or hard-won mastery that makes them capable of surviving the dangers of an adventuring life. Your class determines your Hit Points, your combat capabilities, your magical abilities (if any), and the special features you gain as you advance in level.

## Level 0 and Starting Hit Points

Before any class is chosen, every character has a **Level 0** — the moment before they became an adventurer. At Level 0, your Hit Points equal your **Constitution score** (not your modifier — the score itself). This represents a capable but untempered individual.

Once you take your first class level, your Hit Points advance as normal for that class using its Hit Point Die.

## Choosing a Class

Your class should reflect how your character fights, thinks, and engages with the world. Consider what role you want to fill alongside your companions, what abilities feel true to your character's story, and which subclass speaks to the specific path your character has walked.

## Martials

These classes excel through physical mastery, tactical discipline, or sheer ferocity. They tend to be durable front-liners and reliable damage dealers.

### [Barbarian](./barbarian.md)

*Primary Ability: Strength | Hit Die: D12*

Barbarians are warriors driven by primal Rage — not merely anger, but a fierce and feral force that sharpens their senses, toughens their body, and makes them nearly unstoppable in battle. In Khorvaire, Barbarians might be warriors from the Talenta Plains, savage soldiers from Droaam, Mror Holds clanfighters, or Last War veterans whose years of carnage forged something beyond ordinary courage.

**Subclasses:** [Path of the Berserker](./barbarian.md#path-of-the-berserker), [Path of the Wild Heart](./barbarian.md#path-of-the-wild-heart), [Path of the World Tree](./barbarian.md#path-of-the-world-tree), [Path of the Zealot](./barbarian.md#path-of-the-zealot), [Path of the Demonshard](./barbarian.md#path-of-the-demonshard)

---

### [Fighter](./fighter.md)

*Primary Ability: Strength or Dexterity | Hit Die: D10*

Fighters are the most versatile warriors in Khorvaire — disciplined soldiers, dueling blademasters, psionic warriors, and battle-scarred veterans alike. Every nation fielded its fighters in the Last War, and many now carry their skills into a world scrambling to rebuild.

**Subclasses:** [Battle Master](./fighter.md#battle-master), [Champion](./fighter.md#champion), [Eldritch Knight](./fighter.md#eldritch-knight), [Psi Warrior](./fighter.md#psi-warrior), [Banneret](./fighter.md#banneret)

---

### [Monk](./monk.md)

*Primary Ability: Dexterity and Wisdom | Hit Die: D8*

Monks channel ki — the life force that flows through all living things — into extraordinary physical and spiritual feats. In Khorvaire, monastic traditions are found in the martial disciplines of the Inspired, the meditative paths of Adar, and in rare fighting orders scattered across the continent.

**Subclasses:** [Warrior of Mercy](./monk.md#warrior-of-mercy), [Warrior of Shadow](./monk.md#warrior-of-shadow), [Warrior of the Elements](./monk.md#warrior-of-the-elements), [Warrior of the Open Hand](./monk.md#warrior-of-the-open-hand), [Warrior of the Living Weapon](./monk.md#warrior-of-the-living-weapon)

---

### [Paladin](./paladin.md)

*Primary Ability: Strength and Charisma | Hit Die: D10*

Paladins are sacred warriors bound by oath — to a deity, a cause, or a profound personal conviction. Their martial skill is matched by divine power: they can heal wounds, smite enemies, and shield allies from harm. In Eberron, Paladins serve the Silver Flame, the Sovereign Host, the Blood of Vol, or other powers both recognized and controversial.

**Subclasses:** [Oath of Devotion](./paladin.md#oath-of-devotion), [Oath of Glory](./paladin.md#oath-of-glory), [Oath of the Ancients](./paladin.md#oath-of-the-ancients), [Oath of Vengeance](./paladin.md#oath-of-vengeance), [Oath of the Noble Genies](./paladin.md#oath-of-the-noble-genies)

---

### [Ranger](./ranger.md)

*Primary Ability: Dexterity and Wisdom | Hit Die: D10*

Rangers are hunters and wanderers who have mastered their favored terrain and learned to track, stalk, and survive where others cannot. In Khorvaire, Rangers serve as scouts for the Five Nations, hunters on the Talenta Plains, guides through the Mournland's blasted wastes, or wanderers of the Eldeen Reaches.

**Subclasses:** [Beast Master](./ranger.md#beast-master), [Fey Wanderer](./ranger.md#fey-wanderer), [Gloom Stalker](./ranger.md#gloom-stalker), [Hunter](./ranger.md#hunter), [Winter Walker](./ranger.md#winter-walker), [Bloodhound](./ranger.md#bloodhound)

---

### [Rogue](./rogue.md)

*Primary Ability: Dexterity | Hit Die: D8*

Rogues live by their wits, their speed, and their ability to exploit every advantage. Whether working as thieves, spies, inquisitives, or assassins, they are masters of stealth, precision strikes, and finding the angle others miss. In Khorvaire, Rogues thrive in Sharn's criminal networks, the spy agencies of the Five Nations, or the shadowy employ of the dragonmarked houses.

**Subclasses:** [Arcane Trickster](./rogue.md#arcane-trickster), [Assassin](./rogue.md#assassin), [Soulknife](./rogue.md#soulknife), [Thief](./rogue.md#thief), [Scion of the Three](./rogue.md#scion-of-the-three), [Phantom](./rogue.md#phantom)

## Spellcasters

These classes wield magic as their primary tool — healing, destroying, deceiving, or reshaping the world through arcane or divine power.

### [Artificer](./artificer.md)

*Primary Ability: Intelligence | Hit Die: D8*

Artificers are the magewrights and inventors of Khorvaire — the brilliant minds who cracked open the mysteries of the Draconic Prophecy and turned magic into industry. They bind spells into objects, build mechanical servants, and see every problem as an engineering challenge. The Artificer is Eberron's signature class, at home in a House Cannith workshop or tinkering in the ruins of Xen'drik.

**Subclasses:** [Alchemist](./artificer.md#alchemist), [Armorer](./artificer.md#armorer), [Artillerist](./artificer.md#artillerist), [Battle Smith](./artificer.md#battle-smith), [Cartographer](./artificer.md#cartographer), [Forge Adept](./artificer.md#forge-adept), [Maverick](./artificer.md#maverick)

---

### [Bard](./bard.md)

*Primary Ability: Charisma | Hit Die: D8*

Bards weave magic through performance — music, storytelling, poetry, dance. They are chroniclers, diplomats, enchanters, and inspired virtuosos who inspire allies and unravel enemies with equal skill. In Khorvaire, Bards may be members of the entertainment guilds, traveling performers, kalashtar dream-singers, or scholars of the Pre-Galifar bardic traditions.

**Subclasses:** [College of Dance](./bard.md#college-of-dance), [College of Glamour](./bard.md#college-of-glamour), [College of Lore](./bard.md#college-of-lore), [College of Valor](./bard.md#college-of-valor), [College of Wands](./bard.md#college-of-wands), [College of the Moon](./bard.md#college-of-the-moon), [College of the Dirge Singer](./bard.md#college-of-the-dirge-singer), [College of Whispers](./bard.md#college-of-whispers)

---

### [Cleric](./cleric.md)

*Primary Ability: Wisdom | Hit Die: D8*

Clerics are conduits for divine power — warriors, healers, and scholars of the sacred who channel their deity's will into the world. In Eberron, divine magic is genuine but its sources are complex. Whether serving the Sovereign Host, the Silver Flame, the Blood of Vol, or a more enigmatic power, a Cleric's faith is the engine of their strength.

**Subclasses:** [Life Domain](./cleric.md#life-domain), [Light Domain](./cleric.md#light-domain), [Trickery Domain](./cleric.md#trickery-domain), [War Domain](./cleric.md#war-domain), [Commerce Domain](./cleric.md#commerce-domain), [Knowledge Domain](./cleric.md#knowledge-domain), [Mind Domain](./cleric.md#mind-domain), [Tempest Domain](./cleric.md#tempest-domain)

---

### [Druid](./druid.md)

*Primary Ability: Wisdom | Hit Die: D8*

Druids are the guardians of the natural world, wielding the primal magic of the land, sea, and sky. In Khorvaire, the great druidic sects of the Eldeen Reaches — the Wardens of the Wood, the Ashbound, the Children of Winter — represent living traditions of primal power. Druids may also draw from the forces of the Mournland, Eberron's manifest zones, or the primal mysteries of the daelkyr-touched earth.

**Subclasses:** [Circle of the Land](./druid.md#circle-of-the-land), [Circle of the Moon](./druid.md#circle-of-the-moon), [Circle of the Sea](./druid.md#circle-of-the-sea), [Circle of the Stars](./druid.md#circle-of-the-stars), [Circle of the Forged](./druid.md#circle-of-the-forged)

---

### [Sorcerer](./sorcerer.md)

*Primary Ability: Charisma | Hit Die: D6*

Sorcerers carry magic in their blood — raw, instinctive, sometimes dangerous power that erupts from within rather than being studied or granted. Aberrant dragonmarks, draconic bloodlines, the touch of Xoriat, and the wild chaos of the Mournland have all been known to awaken Sorcerers in Khorvaire.

**Subclasses:** [Aberrant Sorcery](./sorcerer.md#aberrant-sorcery), [Clockwork Sorcery](./sorcerer.md#clockwork-sorcery), [Draconic Sorcery](./sorcerer.md#draconic-sorcery), [Wild Magic Sorcery](./sorcerer.md#wild-magic-sorcery), [Spellfire Sorcery](./sorcerer.md#spellfire-sorcery), [Nemesis Sorcery](./sorcerer.md#nemesis-sorcery)

---

### [Warlock](./warlock.md)

*Primary Ability: Charisma | Hit Die: D8*

Warlocks strike bargains with powerful entities in exchange for magical power. Unlike Wizards who study or Clerics who pray, Warlocks are bound by pact — to an Archfey, a daelkyr, a demon overlord, or something even stranger lurking in the spaces between planes. In Khorvaire, pact-making is a dangerous and often secretive practice.

**Subclasses:** [Archfey Patron](./warlock.md#archfey-patron), [Celestial Patron](./warlock.md#celestial-patron), [Fiend Patron](./warlock.md#fiend-patron), [Great Old One Patron](./warlock.md#great-old-one-patron), [Stone Sovereign Patron](./warlock.md#stone-sovereign-patron), [Undead Patron](./warlock.md#undead-patron)

---

### [Wizard](./wizard.md)

*Primary Ability: Intelligence | Hit Die: D6*

Wizards are the masters of arcane scholarship — they learn magic the hard way, through study, experimentation, and years of dedicated practice. In Khorvaire, Wizards are found in the towers of Arcanix, the research halls of House Cannith and House Sivis, and the independent traditions of Zilargo's gnomish academics.

**Subclasses:** [Abjurer](./wizard.md#abjurer), [Diviner](./wizard.md#diviner), [Evoker](./wizard.md#evoker), [Illusionist](./wizard.md#illusionist), [Bladesinger](./wizard.md#bladesinger)
