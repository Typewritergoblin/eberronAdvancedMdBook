## Skills 
Each ability covers a broad range of capabilities, including skills that a character or a monster can be proficient in. A skill represents a specific aspect of an ability score, and an individual’s proficiency in a skill demonstrates a focus on that aspect. The table below defines the defined skills.

| Skill | Ability | Definition | Typical Uses |
|-------|---------|------------|--------------|
| **Academics** | Intelligence | Your knowledge of scientific principles, mathematics, engineering, and mechanical systems, as well as the ability to analyze complex structures or technical problems. | Identify or analyze mechanical devices; solve mathematical or structural problems; evaluate engineering quality; understand scientific concepts or technical processes; repair or design machines. |
| **Acrobatics** | Dexterity | Your ability to maintain balance and control while moving through unstable or restrictive environments, performing agile movements or avoiding falls. | Balance on narrow or shifting surfaces; perform flips, rolls, or dives; avoid being knocked prone; escape bonds through agile twisting; maneuver through tight spaces. |
| **Animal Handling** | Wisdom | Your ability to read, calm, guide, or command animals through intuition and experience. | Calm frightened animals; direct a mount during hazards; interpret animal moods or intentions; train domesticated creatures; keep mounts steady during chaos. |
| **Arcana** | Intelligence | Your knowledge of magical theory, spellcraft, arcane devices, runes, and planar structure. | Identify spells, magical effects, or arcane items; recall magical traditions; interpret sigils; recognize planar creatures or energies; predict arcane hazards. |
| **Athletics** | Strength | Your physical strength, conditioning, and applied athletic skill used for climbing, running, jumping, swimming, or feats of power. | Climb difficult surfaces; leap long distances; swim through strong currents; resist grappling or forced movement; push, lift, or break objects. |
| **Channel** | Charisma | Your ability to channel or shape raw magical power through force of will, allowing instinctive interaction with magical forces or items. | Activate unstable or unknown magic items; influence wild magic surges; manipulate ambient arcane energy; stabilize magical anomalies; exert will over primal or chaotic magic. |
| **Commune** | Wisdom | Your ability to sense, perceive, and interpret supernatural presences, spiritual echoes, lingering emotions, and extraplanar influences. | Detect or understand spirits, planar creatures, or hauntings; read spiritual residue; interpret omens or subtle supernatural impressions; sense planar thinness or dimensional instability. |
| **Culture** | Intelligence | Your understanding of customs, etiquette, social structures, dialects, and unspoken rules across societies, including access to informal or criminal networks. | Blend into a culture; recall local customs or taboos; find black-market contacts; identify gang symbols or social hierarchies; avoid social offenses; navigate unfamiliar communities. |
| **Deception** | Charisma | Your ability to hide the truth through misleading words, behavior, or expression, ranging from subtle misdirection to outright lies. | Bluff a guard; maintain a false identity; cheat at gambling; disguise motives; convincingly deliver lies; redirect suspicion. |
| **History** | Intelligence | Your knowledge of past events, cultures, conflicts, leadership, migrations, and ancient civilizations. | Recall historical battles; identify relics or symbols of lost cultures; understand political context; recognize ancient lineages; interpret myths or legendary accounts. |
| **Insight** | Wisdom | Your ability to perceive underlying motives, emotions, or intentions through observation and intuition. | Detect lies or hidden motives; sense emotional states; read body language; anticipate someone’s reactions; recognize manipulation. |
| **Intimidation** | Charisma | Your ability to influence others through threats, displays of power, or forceful presence. | Coerce information; scare off attackers; enforce compliance; assert dominance in negotiations; use physical menace. |
| **Investigation** | Intelligence | Your ability to examine clues, analyze patterns, and make logical deductions based on physical or contextual evidence. | Search for hidden objects; deduce what occurred at a scene; identify weak points in structures; interpret coded writing; uncover concealed mechanisms. |
| **Medicine** | Wisdom | Your knowledge of injuries, illnesses, and natural remedies used to diagnose or stabilize the wounded. | Stabilize dying creatures; diagnose diseases; identify poison symptoms; assess wound severity; determine treatment needs. |
| **Nature** | Intelligence | Your knowledge of flora, fauna, geography, weather patterns, and natural cycles. | Identify plants and animals; predict weather; recall natural hazards; understand ecosystems; recognize animal behaviors or tracks. |
| **Perception** | Wisdom | Your awareness of your surroundings and ability to detect hidden or subtle details through sight, sound, or other senses. | Notice hidden creatures; hear faint sounds; spot ambushes; detect minor movements; observe subtle changes in environment. |
| **Performance** | Charisma | Your talent in expressive arts such as music, acting, storytelling, or dance, used to entertain, impress, or influence an audience. | Perform songs or dramatic pieces; entertain crowds; deliver persuasive speeches; distract audiences; express emotion through art. |
| **Persuasion** | Charisma | Your ability to influence others through tact, diplomacy, empathy, or courteous argument. | Negotiate agreements; request aid; calm tensions; inspire groups; gain entry or favors through polite reasoning. |
| **Religion** | Intelligence | Your knowledge of deities, dogma, sacred rites, religious practices, and theological traditions. | Identify holy symbols; recall myths or scripture; interpret omens; understand hierarchy and rituals; recognize divine magic or entities. |
| **Sleight of Hand** | Dexterity | Your ability to perform subtle or deceptive manual movements, allowing for covert manipulation of objects. | Pick pockets; plant items unnoticed; conceal objects; disable traps or locks delicately; palm or switch items covertly. |
| **Stealth** | Dexterity | Your ability to move silently, remain concealed, and avoid drawing attention. | Hide from enemies; move quietly past guards; avoid detection in shadows or crowds; sneak up on targets; slip away unnoticed. |
| **Survival** | Wisdom | Your ability to navigate wilderness, track creatures, avoid natural hazards, and endure harsh environments. | Track creatures; find food and water; predict weather; avoid environmental dangers; guide groups through difficult terrain. |
