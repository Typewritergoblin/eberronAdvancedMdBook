## Skills Description

The table below contains the ability typically tied to each skill, as well as the description and common uses for these skills. At the DM's discretion, different abilities may be substituted for a skill check. 

| Skill | Ability | Definition | Typical Uses |
|-------|---------|------------|--------------|
| **<a id="academics">Academics</a>** | Intelligence | Your knowledge of scientific principles, mathematics, engineering, and mechanical systems, as well as the ability to analyze complex structures or technical problems. | Identify or analyze mechanical devices; solve mathematical or structural problems; evaluate engineering quality; understand scientific concepts or technical processes; repair or design machines. |
| **<a id="acrobatics">Acrobatics</a>** | Dexterity | Your ability to maintain balance and control while moving through unstable or restrictive environments, performing agile movements or avoiding falls. | Balance on narrow or shifting surfaces; perform flips, rolls, or dives; avoid being knocked prone; escape bonds through agile twisting; maneuver through tight spaces. |
| **<a id="animal-handling">Animal Handling</a>** | Wisdom | Your ability to read, calm, guide, or command animals through intuition and experience. | Calm frightened animals; direct a mount during hazards; interpret animal moods or intentions; train domesticated creatures; keep mounts steady during chaos. |
| **<a id="arcana">Arcana</a>** | Intelligence | Your knowledge of magical theory, spellcraft, arcane devices, runes, and planar structure. | Identify spells, magical effects, or arcane items; recall magical traditions; interpret sigils; recognize planar creatures or energies; predict arcane hazards. |
| **<a id="athletics">Athletics</a>** | Strength | Your physical strength, conditioning, and applied athletic skill used for climbing, running, jumping, swimming, or feats of power. | Climb difficult surfaces; leap long distances; swim through strong currents; resist grappling or forced movement; push, lift, or break objects. |
| **<a id="channel">Channel</a>** | Charisma | Your ability to channel or shape raw magical power through force of will, allowing instinctive interaction with magical forces or items. | Activate unstable or unknown magic items; influence wild magic surges; manipulate ambient arcane energy; stabilize magical anomalies; exert will over primal or chaotic magic. |
| **<a id="commune">Commune</a>** | Wisdom | Your ability to sense, perceive, and interpret supernatural presences, spiritual echoes, lingering emotions, and extraplanar influences. | Detect or understand spirits, planar creatures, or hauntings; read spiritual residue; interpret omens or subtle supernatural impressions; sense planar thinness or dimensional instability. |
| **<a id="culture">Culture</a>** | Intelligence | Your understanding of customs, etiquette, social structures, dialects, and unspoken rules across societies, including access to informal or criminal networks. | Blend into a culture; recall local customs or taboos; find black-market contacts; identify gang symbols or social hierarchies; avoid social offenses; navigate unfamiliar communities. |
| **<a id="deception">Deception</a>** | Charisma | Your ability to hide the truth through misleading words, behavior, or expression, ranging from subtle misdirection to outright lies. | Bluff a guard; maintain a false identity; cheat at gambling; disguise motives; convincingly deliver lies; redirect suspicion. |
| **<a id="history">History</a>** | Intelligence | Your knowledge of past events, cultures, conflicts, leadership, migrations, and ancient civilizations. | Recall historical battles; identify relics or symbols of lost cultures; understand political context; recognize ancient lineages; interpret myths or legendary accounts. |
| **<a id="insight">Insight</a>** | Wisdom | Your ability to perceive underlying motives, emotions, or intentions through observation and intuition. | Detect lies or hidden motives; sense emotional states; read body language; anticipate someone’s reactions; recognize manipulation. |
| **<a id="intimidation">Intimidation</a>** | Charisma | Your ability to influence others through threats, displays of power, or forceful presence. | Coerce information; scare off attackers; enforce compliance; assert dominance in negotiations; use physical menace. |
| **<a id="investigation">Investigation</a>** | Intelligence | Your ability to examine clues, analyze patterns, and make logical deductions based on physical or contextual evidence. | Search for hidden objects; deduce what occurred at a scene; identify weak points in structures; interpret coded writing; uncover concealed mechanisms. |
| **<a id="medicine">Medicine</a>** | Wisdom | Your knowledge of injuries, illnesses, and natural remedies used to diagnose or stabilize the wounded. | Stabilize dying creatures; diagnose diseases; identify poison symptoms; assess wound severity; determine treatment needs. |
| **<a id="nature">Nature</a>** | Intelligence | Your knowledge of flora, fauna, geography, weather patterns, and natural cycles. | Identify plants and animals; predict weather; recall natural hazards; understand ecosystems; recognize animal behaviors or tracks. |
| **<a id="perception">Perception</a>** | Wisdom | Your awareness of your surroundings and ability to detect hidden or subtle details through sight, sound, or other senses. | Notice hidden creatures; hear faint sounds; spot ambushes; detect minor movements; observe subtle changes in environment. |
| **<a id="performance">Performance</a>** | Charisma | Your talent in expressive arts such as music, acting, storytelling, or dance, used to entertain, impress, or influence an audience. | Perform songs or dramatic pieces; entertain crowds; deliver persuasive speeches; distract audiences; express emotion through art. |
| **<a id="persuasion">Persuasion</a>** | Charisma | Your ability to influence others through tact, diplomacy, empathy, or courteous argument. | Negotiate agreements; request aid; calm tensions; inspire groups; gain entry or favors through polite reasoning. |
| **<a id="religion">Religion</a>** | Intelligence | Your knowledge of deities, dogma, sacred rites, religious practices, and theological traditions. | Identify holy symbols; recall myths or scripture; interpret omens; understand hierarchy and rituals; recognize divine magic or entities. |
| **<a id="sleight-of-hand">Sleight of Hand</a>** | Dexterity | Your ability to perform subtle or deceptive manual movements, allowing for covert manipulation of objects. | Pick pockets; plant items unnoticed; conceal objects; disable traps or locks delicately; palm or switch items covertly. |
| **<a id="stealth">Stealth</a>** | Dexterity | Your ability to move silently, remain concealed, and avoid drawing attention. | Hide from enemies; move quietly past guards; avoid detection in shadows or crowds; sneak up on targets; slip away unnoticed. |
| **<a id="survival">Survival</a>** | Wisdom | Your ability to navigate wilderness, track creatures, avoid natural hazards, and endure harsh environments. | Track creatures; find food and water; predict weather; avoid environmental dangers; guide groups through difficult terrain. |
