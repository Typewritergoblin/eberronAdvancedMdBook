## Eberron Ability Scores Array

For Eberron, we used "Community Array" - rolling statistics collectively, (all the players rolled 3d6 twice, and we took the top six of them).

**Community Array:** 17 : 15 : 13 : 13 : 11 : 9
**Uncapped Stats:** Caps are uncapped, and can go beyond 20.
**Lvl 0 HP Adjustment:** At level 0, HP is equal to your **constitution score**. Hp advances as normal for classes after that.

## Ability Checks

For most ability checks, I prefer to use a relevant [Skill](../skillsAndSpecializations.md), a [Tool](./toolsProficiency.md), or saving throw. The only exception is **Initiative**, which is a raw Dexterity Check + any Initiative bonus.

## Carrying Capacity

Carrying Capacity is determined by creature **Size** and **Strength** score.

| Size | Carry | Drag / Lift / Push * |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Tiny | STR x 7.5 lbs. | STR x 15 lbs. |
| Small/Medium | STR x 15 lbs. | STR x 30 lbs. |
| Large | STR x 30 lbs. | STR x 60 lbs. |
| Huge | STR x 60 lbs. | STR x 120 lbs. |
| Gargantuan | STR x 120 lbs. | STR x 240 lbs. |
